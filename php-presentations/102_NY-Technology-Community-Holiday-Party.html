Technology Community NYCBUG-NYPHP Holiday Party - MySQL FreeBSD Threads and Captchas

December 7th, 2006

This year, NYCBUG and New York PHP Community are once again in cahoots for a holiday party - with a twist. We'll have two 10 minute hard-nosed presentations to kick things off.

First, NYCBUG's Alfred Perlstein will speak on "Captchas can be LOL."

Then, NYPHP's Hans Zaunere will speak on "Unfashionable FreeBSD: Why Their Threads Are So Last Year."

Then stick around for cash bar and food into the evening. So join us:

  • Suspenders Restaurant

  • Thursday, December 7th, 2006

  • 6:00pm - 9:00pm
