Hi gang,

We just released 4.5.2.3 to, well, um, fix the stuff introduced in the
4.5.2.2 patch :oops:

In the attempts to better safeguard against some known XSS attacks, we
filtered out a little too much stuff when content gets stored into the
database. An example is PayPal donation buttons, which have javascript
form actions - those were accidentally getting pruned.

If you have a running 4.5.x install, then applying the patch will give
you the latest and greatest.

For new installs, the 4.5.2.3 tarball is suggested.

Thanks and happy mambo-ing!
