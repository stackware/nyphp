Hope that you are all enjoying the holidays.

I want to share a present with you.

Last night I received a message from RedComponent, the makers of RedShop,
offering all their extensions for free. (I am sure that many of you who are
registered at RedComponent to download jUpgrade received the same message.)

I visited RedComponent.com and discovered that you can download their
extensive extension product line, including RedShop, RedForm, RedEvent and
RedMember for free!

Now, don't get too excited because RedComponent is still charging for the
add-on modules, like shipping and payment gateways for RedShop, but most
are reasonably priced at $25 each. (Plus there is an additional 25%
discount.)

I have personally never used RedShop, but I have used RedForm (similar to
BreezyForms) and RedCompetition (a neat quiz/test component) and found both
to be of excellent quality. Tech support is also good, but I am not sure
how support will work for those products that they are giving away.

Earlier this year Scott Wolpow gave an overview of RedShop and liked it so
you are planning an e-commerce project, you might want to check it out.

And I want to wish Merry Christmas and Happy Hanukkah to everyone. 2013
looks like it's going to be a great year for Joomla! and Joomla NYC.

All best,

Steve
