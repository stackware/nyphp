I have a Tumblr website I want to move to Joomla 2.5.8. I'm thinking about
how to duplicate the functionality of the blog. I'm not interested in
having the blog from Tumblr appear on a Joomla website, this is about doing
a total migration to Joomla from Tumblr. Tumblr has been good, but this
website needs things which Joomla does very well.

I have some thoughts about how to do what Tumblr did in Joomla and wanted
to share them to see if others have suggestions of things I might be
overlooking.

Tumblr main features:
Post a blog
Post a video
Post a photo
Post at a scheduled time
Tags
Ability to Share specific postings

For Joomla, each blog entry could be an Article. It would be posted in
chronology order with the most recent postings showing at the top on the
home page.

Any other tips or comments about this? Am I missing something from Tumblr
that needs to be done in Joomla? Thanks!
