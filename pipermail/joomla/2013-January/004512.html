Thanks for the feedback, Marcos. :-)

 

Here's what you need to do to get your content imported from Artisteer:

 

1.       In the Administrator area  of your site, go to the template manager
(Extensions.Template Manager)

2.       Click on your template name to edit the Artisteer template.

3.       On the right side, under Basic Options, click the "Import Content
from Template" button.

4.       You'll get a warning message that says: 
 This option will import articles, extensions and menus, and will create a
new homepage.
Note: Previously imported content will be replaced, therefore please back it
up if required.

5.       Click the Import button to start the import.

6.       Once it's complete you'll get the message "Content is installed
successfully."

7.       Click Continue button.

8.       Click Save & Close button.

 

That's it!  Let me know how it works out.
