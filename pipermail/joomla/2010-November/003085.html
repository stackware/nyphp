This depends on the markup you are using: Most commen markups are WML or
XHTML MP. You can find a good introduction into creating web content at
http://www.oreillynet.com/wireless/2004/02/06/mobile_browsing.html

For WML a typical header would be:

<?xml version="1.0"?>
<!DOCTYPE wml PUBLIC "-//WAPFORUM//DTD WML 1.1//EN"
"http://www.wapforum.org/DTD/wml_1.1.xml">
