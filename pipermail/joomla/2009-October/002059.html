Leam,
I used to host my joomla! sites on godaddy and experienced nothing but a lot of problems...I would really encourage your client if you can to migrate to a different host...not only did I have issues because of the godaddy servers, but their support wasn't good if it had anything to do with joomla.

I am sure the group can recommend other inexpensive options...I like hostgator.com, but listen to all of the suggestions, I am sure youwill find the right way to go...
