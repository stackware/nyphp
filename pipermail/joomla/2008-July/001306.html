I like Joomlart templates. Joomlart offers a subscription so you can 
download several and try them. The templates usually come with Photoshop 
files to modify the default graphics, and custom CSS classes for 
formatting body text. I have also used Joomlashack templates. They are 
very well done, however, I believe you have to pay for each one 
separately - even a color variation on a single template.
