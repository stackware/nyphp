Hi Donna,
You may want to check out this extension. 
http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/5694/details  I believe it will 
automatically resize any image before it is displayed in an article and 
create a copy of the original image in the specified size.

You may also like this extension: 
http://extensions.joomla.org/extensions/core-enhancements/file-management/4813/details 
it automatically resizes any images in a directory, and can be used for 
front-endk uploading.

Good luck,
