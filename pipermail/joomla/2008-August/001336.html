On Aug 19, 2008, at 12:34 PM, Donna Marie Vincent wrote:

> In your .htaccess file, search for "RewriteEngine" and see if it  
> says "on" or "off" next to it.  If it has a "#" in front of it,  
> delete that.  So, if you see this:
>
> # RewriteEngine on
>
> change it to:
>
> RewriteEngine on
>
> Or if you see:
>
> RewriteEngine off
>
> Change it to
>
> RewriteEngine on
>
>
> If you don't find it in the file at all, then just type it in:
>
> RewriteEngine on
> RewriteCond %{http_host} ^muchospanish\.com [nc]
> RewriteRule ^(.*)$ http://www.muchospanish.com/$1 [R=permanent,nc,L]
>
