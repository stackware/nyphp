I just figured out something which has bugged me for a while.

On the Joomla extentions directory, there is a little link to make an 
extention a "favorite"

If you go to "My Page" in the left hand menu, on your user profile are 
sections for extentions you have posted, extentions you have reviewed, 
and extentions you have flagged as a favorite.

So if you remember to flag extentions you want to come back to as a 
favorite, it will be in that list.
