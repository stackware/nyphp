There are a bunch of Joomla extensions that can add fields to Joomla.

The most popular is K2 at http://getk2.org

And there is also Zoo by YooTheme, Seblod and ContentBuilder from CrhssTec
http://crosstec.de/en/  and more.

I am also hopeful that adding fields, and comments will be part of Joomla
2.5, but I did not see them in the first beta of Joomla 2.5. Maybe next
time.
