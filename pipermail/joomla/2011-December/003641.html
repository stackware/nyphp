Greetings!

This is a reminder that the December Joomla NYC meeting takes place
tomorrow (12/08/11) at 6:30 PM at Lincoln Center, 146 West 65th Street. For
directions, consult www.joomlanyc.org

I recently heard from Scott Wolpow that he will be unable to attend the
meeting and give his GIT presentation.

However, I will present the K2 component and discus other CCKs (Content
Construction Kit) that can be used with Joomla. And Laura Gordon will
demonstrate how to use Firebug and Firefox. As well as the Lightning Round,
where you can bring your questions and get answers from Joomla experts.

Admission is free and there will be snacks and bottled water.

Hope to see everyone tomorrow night!
