Hey gang,

The new name is........................Joomla!

Yep, Joomla! is the official name for the new and improved Mambo.
After receiving thousands of ideas and concepts from the community, we
brought in some professionals to whittle down the list to the most
attractive choices.

This actually was the only one that passed the Trademark test, and
didn't mean something profane in French.

You can read all about it at our new website (in the process of being
ground to a pulp):
