>     Having used 1&1 in the past, and having sites hacked when using
>     them, I changed to a different host and most of my security issues
>     when away. I now host my own sites on servers that I build and
>     stop most exploits long before they even get to the website. Any
>     good host will do that too.
>
>     Make sure all _*extensions*_ are up to date, not listed at
>     http://docs.joomla.org/Vulnerable_Extensions_List and passwords
>     are strong. I know that /administrator on my servers get hit with
>     dictionary attacks
>     <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dictionary_attack> 100's of times a
>     day and using Admin Tools secret URL helps with that.
>
>     But, your host needs to be doing something better to prevent this
>     behavior before it gets to your site.
>
