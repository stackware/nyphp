> However, the organization has been having some DNS issues, plus has the

> server.  Thus at times, strange HTTP hits with the wrong or no virtual host

You're pretty much forced to do some redirects, and setting up named
vhosts goes a long way to making sure the correct requests go to the
correct place - and therefore the correct URLs get cached.

configuration.php, but in 1.5 the fancypants developers outnumbered
