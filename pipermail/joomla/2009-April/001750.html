The power of Joomla! with Drupal configurability

Joopal permits you to use your traditional Drupal blocks anywhere a Joomla!
module can do and also allows you to integrate Drupal's modules as if they
were a normal Joomla! component. This permits your Joomla! site to pick and
choose the features and functionality you need against two of the world's
most popular content management systems!
http://community.joomla.org/blogs/leadership/780-introducing-joopal.html
