>From www.joomla.org:

"Joomla! has been awarded two prestigious awards at the Linux & Open
Source Awards in London.

The Joomla! project won "Best Linux / Open Source Project" for 2005.

Joomla! project lead Andrew Eddie said the award demonstrates the
level of faith and support the Open Source community has in Joomla!.

"The past two months have been astounding," Mr Eddie said. "To win
this award on the back of our first releases means a lot to the team
which has worked so hard for the community."

The second award which Joomla! figures in was won by core member Brian
Teeman. Brian won "UK Individual Contribution to Open / Source" for
2005.

Mr Teeman said he was honoured to win the award and said it recognised
"you don't have to be a coder to contribute to the world of Open
Source".

More news from London soon..."
