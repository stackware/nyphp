The next regular meeting of the New York City Joomla User Group will be

Thursday March 20, 2014

 

Location:

Touro College ( ... more about Touro College)

Graduate School of Business

65 Broadway, Room 224 (map )

between Rector & Exchange Place

New York, NY 10006

Registration is not requred, but it would be nice if you clicked the link on
the www.joomlanyc.org homepage to signup for the event on meetup.com

 

 

Chairperson: Stewart Newfeld

6:00 - 6:15 - Introductions

6:15 - 8:00

 

Presentation 1: Mark Simko will report about the March 15-16 Boston
JoomlaDay. Joomla was the first popular CMS with responsive design, first
with MVC, and now the first in Composer.  Come find out what Composer is,
why that matters, and what's the buzz from JoomlaDay.

 

Other Presentations: Stewart Newfeld has been working extensively with
RSForm!Pro and Chronoforms the two most popular Joomla forms extensions.
He'll cover some Intermediate level issues with RSForm!Pro and answer
questions about developing forms in Joomla.

 

8:00-8:30 Lightening Round Question and Answers, Networking.

 

 

Stewart Newfeld, President
