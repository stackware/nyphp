The next regular meeting of the New York City Joomla Users group  will be
held on
Thursday January 16, 2014.  Admission is free.  

Advanced signup isn't required but the meeting is listed on meetup.com and
we'd appreciate you signing up and letting us know you will be attending at
http://www.meetup.com/New-York-City-Joomla-Users-Group/events/159871022/



Location:
Touro College ( ... more about Touro College
<http://joomlanyc.org/index.php/events/touro-college-our-meetings-host> )
Graduate School of Business (Please note: No food or drink other than water
is allowed in the Touro College classrooms.)
65 Broadway, Room 224 (map  <http://www.touro.edu/gsb/contact.html> )
between Rector & Exchange Place
New York, NY 10006

Chairperson: Stewart Newfeld 
6:00 - 6:15 - Introductions
6:15 - 8:00 - Presentation:
Mark Simko of Digifix will present on SEBLOD
SEBLOD is a Web Application Builder, and Content Construction Kit for
Joomla! Their website says:
"Do not think Drupal or Wordpress anymore!  Think Joomla + SEBLOD & create
the Web 3.0!"
Website: http://www.seblod.com/

8:00-8:30 Lightening Round Question and Answers, Networking.

This semester Touro College is once again kindly providing the NYC Joomla
Users Group with facilities for our meetings on the 3rd Thursday of every
month including  January 16, February 20, and March 20.

 

Stewart Newfeld, President
