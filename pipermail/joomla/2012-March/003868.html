 Next NYC Joomla Group meeting. No reservations required. And we feed you
too. Thursday March 8, 2012

Chairperson: Scott Wolpow
*Meeting*: 6:30-8:30, NEW LOCATION! 146 West 65th Street. The entrance is
across the street from the stairs leading to our old meeting space in Rose
Hall. You will see the double-doors underneath the fancy glass-enclosed
restaurant on the main concourse. Go through the doors and walk to the back
and you will see signs leading to the conference room.
*Networking: *8:30-9:30
*Presentations*:
*Scott Wolpow: *Getting the most from Git. Git is an uber popular
distributed version control system used by many open source software
developers to share code with others. Git was originally developed by Linus
Torvalds, founding developer of the Linux operating system.
*Stephen Britton*: An overview of
ContentBuilder<http://crosstec.de/en/extensions/joomla-cck-download.html>,
a CCK component by Crosstec, a company better known for its Breezy Forms
extension. ContentBuilder provides an easy way to display content from CSV
files on websites. He will demonstrate how to import content from Excel
spreadsheets and put it on the web.
*Plus: *Yet-to-be determined SWAG, and as always, the infamous Lightning
Round - we provide answers to your Joomla questions.
