*What is Joomla?*

Joomla is an Open Source Content Management System to easily administer 
websites and add content. It is simple enough for the layperson and 
robust enough to build complex sites. Best of all , it is free to use.

*Who uses Joomla?*

*Who should attend?*

*Business Owners*- Tracks will include not only how to do it yourself, 
but how to hire and manage a company to build your site

*Non-Profits*- Learn how Joomla can help give you a quality web presence 
at an affordable cost

*Newbies*- Just starting? Learn the basics

*Intermediate Users-* Go beyond the base install and bring the site to a 
new level

*Experienced Users*- Take your skills to the next level --Learn how to 
create components

*Magazine Publishers*- perfect tool to bring your publications online.

*Graphic Artists*- Take your designs and apply them to a template

*SEO/SEM*- learn how to apply these skills to Joomla
