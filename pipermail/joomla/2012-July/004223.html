Hi Janet.

Hope you are having a great summer.

It looks like you are doing everything right. When I use captcha-recaptcha
I just check the box in the Global Configuration panel and turn on the
plug-in with the appropriate public/private keys from the Recaptcha website.

I would check your settings with the ones on Steve Burge's captcha tutorial.
http://www.ostraining.com/blog/joomla/captcha-joomla/

Also, is this site running on your local PC/Mac using WAMP, MAMP. XAMPP,
etc. or is it on a live site? I have heard that the captcha feature does
not work well on local servers. If possible, try to test your settings on a
live site.

All best,

Steve
