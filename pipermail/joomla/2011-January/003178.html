I took a look and it's not clear if and how it can be used in other
languages than english.This is important to me as my target audience is not
english speaking.

Some international payment methods are missing. Also important, because my
target audiences uses a local payment method that offers services that
PayPal won't.

There's a monthly fee to use the service. It's not much, still, it's there.
And by being commercial, it lacks the appeal Joomla has.

As Donna mentioned, her friend is graphic artist. Perhaps she is comfortable
with a solution that won't need the knowledge of programming. I'm a graphic
designer and I relate to that. I can't create code, but I feel comfortable
looking into it and changing what I need to.

Adobe BC seems to be a threat to Joomla just like any other CMS out there.

2010/12/15 Donna Marie Vincent <donnamarievincent at yahoo.com>
