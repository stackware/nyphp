There are products out there that send RSS feeds to twitter (you've probably
seen them clogging up popular hashtags). Can come across as spammy, but it
works.

http://www.google.com/search?q=rss+to+twitter

Facebook will allow RSS too:
http://www.ehow.com/how_2031209_import-rss-feed.html

Digg is harder - they'll ban you pretty fast for automated submissions.
