Depending on what version of Joomla your using, that can be a fairly 
simple plugin - not a component.  You just need to grab the save user 
event and check the password at that point.  I'd try to avoid the no 
reuse though, it's a real pain and just leads to users gaming the 
system[once I know the password cycle is X passwords, I just make X 
updates until I can set my preferred password].

I imagine there already is a plugin for this, just check the extensions 
directory.
