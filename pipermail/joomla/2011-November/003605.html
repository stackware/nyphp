Good afternoon!

Just a friendly reminder that the November Joomla NYC User Group meeting
takes place at 6:30 PM tomorrow (Thursday, 11/10/11) at Lincoln Center, 146
West 65th Street. (For better directions see www.joomlanyc.org ).

Presentations this month include:

* Joomla Day NYC Weekend 2011 Wrap Up.

* Stewart Newfield will recap his favorite presentations at Joomla Day
Weekend.

* Scott Wolpow will explain Joomla's Uniform Content Model is plain talk.

* Joomla NYC Board of Directors nominations. Your chance to be on the board
and promote Joomla!

* As always, the infamous Lightning Round. Bring your Joomla questions and
ask the group for answers.

Hope to see ya'll tomorrow evening!
