When I tested Coda I discovered something that I could not live with -  
after selecting a line of code or just a word, it was not easy to then  
drag that highlighted text to another location on the page. The  
pointer had to be positioned perfectly or the text would become  
deselected on mouse down. Maybe this has been fixed?

Other than that gripe, it is quality software for middle and frontend  
development. The integration of the terminal, css, ftp, etc is nice  
for keeping an uncluttered desktop. But that said, it did not seem to  
have enough innovations to make me want to buy it.
