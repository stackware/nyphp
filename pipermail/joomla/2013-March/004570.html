JCE has a major advantage / feature, that no other editor I have found has.

In JCE, you can configure the available styles, to make use of the styles
in your site's template.  So if you define some custom styles in the CSS of
your template, you can use it in JCE.

You cannot use this in JCK, meaning you cannot see how your articles look
without saving them and looking them up on the web, first.
