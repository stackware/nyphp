I have a few clients using Joomla on GoDaddy and I find their sites to be very slow.  Also GoDaddy has a cached server so changes you make to an .htaccess file can take up to an hour for you to see the results.  I like, use and recommend Hostgator for Joomla sites, plus their customer service is pretty good too.
