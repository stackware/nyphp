We were unable to gain access to Robert Half's office due to an administrative snafu, so we walked over to Bryant Park and had our meeting al fresco and with free Wi-Fi!  The weather was perfect for an outdoor meeting, and we snacked on $8 blue corn chips and chocolate rugala.  :-)

Thanks all!  I enjoyed our meeting.
