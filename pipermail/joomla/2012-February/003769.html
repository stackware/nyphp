I have tried several different hosting companies over the years (since 2004) and the one I keep coming back to is Hostgator.  I have been disappointed by all the others.  The only other one I like is Imageleet if you need hosting that supports video processing.  Hostgator's support is very responsive and is available 24/7 via online chat and email.  And the pricing is excellent.
