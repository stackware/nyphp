AIP-New York is proud to present:

***Internet Jobs: Today and Tomorrow.***
[An event for hiring managers and professional talent]

**Jan 22, 2003

Location: IBM Building, 590 Madison Ave, New York. (Cross at 57th St.)

Time: 6:00 PM Jan 22, 2003 for food and networking. Event starts at
6:30.

Price: $15 at the door, Free for members, $10 for early RSVP.

Space is limited and running out. Please RSVP to reserve your space.

** To RSVP for this event:
Email: dseligson at association.org


Special Event Feature: Personal Coaching by Oren Klaber.

Submit your resume to be reviewed by Oren Klaber, CEO of TMP Worldwide,
the parent company of Monster.com. Two winners will be announced
at the event and brought up on stage for a live coaching session
in front of the audience.
To submit your resume for coaching, email it to:
Dseligson at association.org

** Speakers:

Keynote: Oren Klaber, CEO of  TMP Worldwide.
TMP Worldwide, owns Monster.com and is an interactive company with a
unique
suite of Global Career Solutions and a client base that includes
more than 90 of the Fortune 100 and more than 490 of the Fortune 500.

At TMP's core is their internet-based "Intern to CEO" strategy,
which drives their capabilities into a one-of-a-kind employment
continuum with the Internet at its center. This continuum is the
framework for many of TMP's core competencies:
Monster, Advertising & Communications, Executive Search,
and eResourcing Speakers will be discussing:

- Where the jobs are today
- Where demand is growing for talent
- Trends in the workplace
- Tactics For Hiring Managers
- Tactics For Candidates

Each speaker will have 20 minutes to present their view of the trends
in the employment market and will respond to questions. In addition,
2 attendees will have the opportunity to be coached in front of
the audience by Oren Klaber.

100-200 attendees will be expected. These will be largely managers
and executives with IT, Marketing, and Design backgrounds. Resumes will
be
taken from all attendees, to be given to recruiters. Tables will be
arranged for hiring managers to meet attendees. Attendees and sponsors
will
have free access to post and receive positions and resumes from the job
board
of the AIP-NYC website. Complimentary soft drinks and food will be
served.


** About AIP

The Association of Internet Professionals, is the largest professional
association in the Internet industry, and includes professionals ranging

from the developer to the CEO, the marketing and salesperson to the
graphic designer, and the copywriter to the webmaster.

AIP exists to help its members succeed in business. Its mission,
"to support, unify and represent the global community of Internet
professionals" is carried out through benefits programs, networking and
topical discussion opportunities (both online and through regional
chapters),
policy advocacy and special projects, such as the AIP Compensation &
Benefits
Report and the AIP Certification Accreditation Council
(www.accredit.net).

Learn more about the AIP at http://www.association.org
