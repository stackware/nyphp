	I wish this job were more an entirely "PHP Job", but
it isn't.  It is probably more of an "HTML job," so some graphics

databases (Access, MySQL) and e-commerce solutions (Yahoo Store,
InterShop, etc.).  Perl and PHP abilities would be the next
priority, I would suppose.
