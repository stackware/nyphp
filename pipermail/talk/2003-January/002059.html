Greetings all,

The planning for NYPHP's presence at the LinuxWorld Expo is rapidly 
progressing.  The best way to keep tabs on our preparation is to sign up for 
the relatively low traffic NYPHP Development list and the UniLUG Expo 
mailing list: [http://nyphp.org/list/] and 
[http://unilug.org/mailinglists.html]

We're currently looking for volunteers to provide a digital camera or video 
recording equipment so that we can record the Q & A session with Zend 
Technologies CEO Doron Gerstel on Wednesday the 22nd.

If anybody is willing to help record the event or just to provide the 
necessary equipment for the afternoon, please get in touch with Hans or I as
