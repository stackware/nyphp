> This seems to work:

> mysql> SELECT 'The West and a Western South-westerly Revolt' REGEXP 
> mysql> BINARY
> '[[:<:]]West[[:>:]]' as matches;
>

> | matches |
> +---------+
> |       1 |
> +---------+
