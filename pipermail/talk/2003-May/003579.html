Why would you want to do it in the query? Formatting, like color, 
should really be done in the presentation layer. For my purposes I 
create 10 classes in my style sheets so that I can hilight up 10 search 
words in different colors. I then do a search and replace on the words 
so that "dynamics" would be changed to <span 
class="searchword1">dynamics</span>. This won't interfere with any of 
you other formatting or color schemes you have in place.
