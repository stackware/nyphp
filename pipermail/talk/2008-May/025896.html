Your situation isn't something that I've done before, so I can't
really help you other than pointing you in the (hopefully) right
direction. Don't be scared of mod_rewrite, there are plenty of people
on this list and elsewhere that can answer questions.

I forgot that Apache's mod_proxy is too dumb to rewrite the links.
There is a Free module called mod_proxy_html that will:
http://apache.webthing.com/mod_proxy_html/

If you can use that instead of the default mod_proxy it will make
things much easier, because then the internal servers can stay hidden.
At that point, you could even configure them to only answer to www.
