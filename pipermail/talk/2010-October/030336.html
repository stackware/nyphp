I haven't seen a php function to do it (not to say one doesn't exist).  However, I use sphinx for search and there's a unicode to latin post in their wiki that might help you: http://sphinxsearch.com/wiki/doku.php?id=charset_tables#slovak  With a related post here - http://darwinweb.net/articles/thinking-sphinx-case-folding-configuration
