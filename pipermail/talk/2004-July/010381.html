Hi:

There's a free drop in alternative for PGP called GPG (GNU Privacy Guard),
found at http://www.gnupg.org/.

Since you're talking about using this for email, check the "MUA Frontend"
(mail user agent) section of the "Related Software" | "Frontend" page
http://www.gnupg.org/(en)/related_software/frontends.html#mua .  There are
even plugins for Outlook.
