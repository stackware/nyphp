I have been using Dreamhost because it's cheap and provides what I need and
has a really weird and funny newsletter.

However, if you need a custom php.ini, the instructions are found at
http://www.wiki.dreamhost.com/index.php/PHP.ini with a great big disclamer
to the effect that if it suddenly breaks, you're on your own. Sure enough,
my little php app suddenly broke. Tore my hair out for a while, then finally
worked around.

Is the ability to customize your php.ini asking too much of a low-end
shared-hosting provider?
