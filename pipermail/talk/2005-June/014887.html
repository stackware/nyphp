Two good screen recording applications:

Camtasia: mutliple output options, including flash, quicktime and windows
media.  Also has "full frame" capture (true video card output capture, full
motion video):
http://www.techsmith.com/products/studio/default.asp


Quaron Viewlet Builder:  only flash output, only "keyframe" images, but
works really well and is less expensive (slightly) than camtasia.  
http://www.qarbon.com/

I have used both extensively.  Viewlet builder output is very lean and fast,
but authoring can be kind of frustrating.  Camtasia is good but does not
optimize movie size like Viewlet can.
