COBOL type example below is it possible to set up group data structures as 
illustrated by ACCOUNT and NAME?



01                  EXAMPLE-RECORD.                 SYNC.

05      ACCOUNT.

10        BRANCH                  PIC 9(6).

10        ACCOUNT-NO        PIC 9(8).

10        PIN                             PIC 9(4).

05    BALANCE                            PIC S9(6)v99        COMP-3

05      NAME.

10         GIVEN                       PIC X(25).

10         SURNAME              PIC X(30).

05    FILLER                                  PIC X(40).
