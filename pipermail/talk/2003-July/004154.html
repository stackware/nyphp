It all depends on what your interests are actually.
Look for researches that the faculty are doing and see
if you want to be part of it. The websites of the
school department is a good start. Look also into the
tools and technology these researches are using. Stick
to the open source technologies.

Math expertise or the lack of it will factor in
depending on the specific field you want to go into. I
guess what I am saying is you have to know where to
start and proceed from there. It will also determine
if you want to get "married" into it.

Hope this helps.
