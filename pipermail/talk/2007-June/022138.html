I definitely feel that "Reply" should go to sender and "Reply To All"
to group as that seems to be the way it's _supposed_ to work, but I'm

conversation, which, it seems, is the point.

used things as they were built, we'd all be out of a job and hence
wouldn't have very much to talk about here.
