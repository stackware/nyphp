I agree and understand, but also think that it benefits companies to 
separate out these duties.  A programmer is usually a very substandard 
graphic designer, and vice versa.  It's very fortunate that your 
company had the luck to find two programmers who not only are competent 
doing Production work but don't mind doing it...they may find their 
luck changing if the job market picks up methinks :)
