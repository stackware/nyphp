Well, we've (finally) got a fully functional public demo of Andromeda 
out there:

http://www.andromeda-project.org/demo

It is the start of a basic accounting system, with about a dozen tables 
populated with test data.  Feel free to insert, delete, etc.

The entire app has only 5 source files, and you can view them right 
after logging in.  The largest of course is the bookkeep.dd.yaml file, 
the heart of the whole thing.
