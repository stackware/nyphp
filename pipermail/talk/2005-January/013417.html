> I know this comes up from time to time on this list, but I recall
> someone on here being affiliated with Zend, and offering discounts for
> their products. I've been playing around with Zend Studio lately, and
> might be interested in purchasing a few licenses. Anyone have any info
> about it?

As a beta tester you get a pretty good amount off the price (20%?).  they
just started a round of testing for ZDE 4, so you might be able to get in on
it.

> Also, any comments about their support? Are you "on your own" after you
> buy the product, or is their support top notch? Any thoughts or opinions
> are greatly appreciated!

I've had very good experience with their support, at least in the one
instance I truly needed it.  There were some shared servers the ftp of ZDE
wouldn't log into correctly.  It wouldn't show linked directories properly.
I gave the tech a login to recreate the error and within a couple days I had
a new build of ZDE 3 which fixed the problem.
