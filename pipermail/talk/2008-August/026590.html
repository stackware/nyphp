I recently saw a film at the Quad Cinema called "Stealing America: Vote 
by Vote" which did a really excellent job of explaining how the voting 
machines "work" (or don't work, depending on how you look at it), and 
even interviewed some of the guys who were involved in the programming 
of the voting machines.  They got as technical as they could for a 
sophisticated general audience.

recommend that anyone interested in this issue see the film.
