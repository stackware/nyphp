Did you figure this one out?

Try

$data - file_get_contents($some_remote_https_url);
$pieces = explode(',', data);
print preg_replace("/(\\\x[0-9A-Fa-f]{1,2})/e", "chr(hexdec('\\1'))", $pieces[0]);

Seems to do the trick.
