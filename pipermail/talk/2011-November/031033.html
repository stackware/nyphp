What is New York City Webgrrls?
Webgrrls International is a community of professional women who leverage
technology to become more successful in their careers and businesses. With
Technology changing at the speed of light, women from all walks of life join
webgrrls to ³stay in the know², discuss the latest tech trends, support each
other through business and technology issues, and develop new business
contacts and friends. Members can connect online or in person at a local
chapter by attending monthly meetings and workshops.
