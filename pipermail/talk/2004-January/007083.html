I've done things like this a couple of ways. One was for a radio show producer who wanted a long list of radio stations and times for the show he produced updated on the site all the time. He kept the info in Excel. You can save Excel as an HTML fragment (no opening or closing), so I had him do that, upload it through a form, and used it as an include.

Alternately, you could just have a password protected form where he could edit a text file.
