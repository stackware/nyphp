Hey folks,

An article I wrote for IBM's developerWorks site went live this week with 
the May issue of the IBM WebSphere Developer Technical Journal.

The article argues that while PHP and WebSphere are generally considered 
competitors, they can work together to support a common type of 
application.  The article covers the roles of a web application, where PHP 
and WebSphere fit in, and how to install them together with Apache and DB2 
on a Linux server. 

I also provided a sample application as a proof of concept.   The example 
code consists of a J2EE Struts application which serves as a content 
management system to a PHP-driven public website.

Enjoy, and don't forget to submit a rating at the bottom of the article - 
if that module shows up for you... I seem to have been spared of it for 
now :)
