In this case, his was in Microsoft U-law format..
lame expects PCM so you just do
`sox foo.wav -f bar.wav`
Which converts it to PCM then he can feed it through lame just fine.

Ref: http://www.visual-mp3.com/mp3-to-wav/wav-format.html
