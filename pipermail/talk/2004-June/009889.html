I'm completely out of my league here (CGI), but have you tried using a PassEnv
directive in httpd.conf?

http://httpd.apache.org/docs/mod/mod_env.html#passenv

Something like "PassEnv TEMP" should make $_SERVER['TEMP'] available in PHP.

See also:
http://httpd.apache.org/docs/env.html
