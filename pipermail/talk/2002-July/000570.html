I don't know the LI trains very well, but from looking at:

http://mta.info/lirr/html/lirrmap.htm

maybe something like this would work:

Babylon Branch -> Hicksville -> Port Jefferson Branch  -> Jamaica ->
NYC Transit (E/J/Z)? (http://mta.info/nyct/maps/submap.htm) -> Penn
Station

Once at Penn Station, take the C or E to 23rd street, then either a bus
or walk east to Digital Pulp (map available at http://nyphp.org).

I think there's a subway that's more direct from Jamaica to 23rd
Street, but I'm not sure - maybe someone else knows better about this.
