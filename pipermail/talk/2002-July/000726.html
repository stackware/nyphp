Anyone know of some good free (or very cheap) software for inventory 
management?

I have a potential client who has this problem: their current system handles 
the inventory count OK (decrementing inventory when an order is shipped), 
but it is not very flexible and does not handle other needs.  They also need 
some way to note, for example, that item a is selling at a rate of b units 
per day, that they will be out-of-stock at the current rate in c days, that 
person d has put in an order on date e for f amount of new stock, that the 
new stock is estimated to arrive on date g, and that in-stock item h (maybe 
a different color of the same type of item or some other minor variation) 
should be pushed by sales as an alternative if item a is out-of-stock.
