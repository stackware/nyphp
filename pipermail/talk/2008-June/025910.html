I think your first step is to define what a CMS is, at least for your 
purposes, and why the existing open source wheels need to be reinvented 
again.  From an ERD perspective, I'd design the data store to be 
polymorphic which the application would aggregate as a set of nodes 
defining a blog article, a comment, a forum post, etc.
