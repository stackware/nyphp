Just wanted to jump in here about something Mark said in the message quoted
below: If you have the ability to take the site down and are thinking about
doing so I would definitely talk to an attorney before you do anything like
that.

A few months ago, I got stiffed on a $3200 contract and I was seriously
considering doing the exact same thing. I found out though that, unless your
contract has very specific language that allows you to do so, taking the
site down can result in you being successfully sued. Especially if they are
making money on it.

Talk to an attorney before you do anything so drastic. It sucks not getting
paid but being sued sucks more.
