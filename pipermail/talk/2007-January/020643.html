OFF-TOPIC

US Federal taxes currently vary from 28 to 38 percent and that's not 
counting the 15.2 required FICA tax that you and your employer share 
in paying. Nor does it cover the cost of preparing taxes (i.e., tax 
preparer, book keeper, the time you waste).

I figured it up one day and after all the Federal, State, and Local 
taxes we pay in the US, we're lucky to keep 30-40 percent of what we 
earn.

Back before President Reagan, I was forced to immediately (within 3 
days) pay 50 percent of what I made to the Federal government. The 
combination of federal and local taxes were so bad that some 
California entertainers (Arthur Godfrey for one) were required to pay 
105 percent of their earnings. Only government can think that it's 
fair to tax you more than what you make.
