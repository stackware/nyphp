Have you looked at LiveHTTPHeaders? It's a Firefox plugin rather than a 
proxy, but it's good enough for most situations. I find it a tad 
frustrating when I need to view the content, and there are some other 
things that annoy me, but it's worth checking out.

There's also this: http://sourceforge.net/projects/protoscope/

I wrote it back in the late 90s and reworked it a bit a few years later. 
It's nothing great, but it works most of the time. :-)
