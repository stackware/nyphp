Stephen,

I am currently the data-migration lead on a massive project covering just over 5,000 tables. I found the most effective tools to be Access, for getting a handle and feel on the data..
Visio, for setting up the normalized state of the 'new' DBs... Great DDL export features and options.
Query Analyzer, for actually scrubbing and working with the 'to-be moved' data.  

I'm fortunate enough to have an employer who provides me with this tools, however I have heard some really good things about DDT which can be found here amongst others:
http://www.databaseanswers.com/modelling_tools.htm
