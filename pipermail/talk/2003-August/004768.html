If you have items left over, you may want to donate the rest to one of the
non-profits here in NYC that are helping other non-profits use technology.
Check out npowerny.org... they are a good organizationa and always looking
for old machines for inner city labs.

And if you should throw any electronics away, be sure they don't go to the
landfill due to all the toxic contents.  Check department of sanitation
web site for proper disposal.
