Linux and the BSDs)
http://developer.apple.com/internet/macosx/cvsoverview.html

http://linux.oreillynet.com/pub/a/linux/2002/01/03/cvs_intro.html

http://linux.oreillynet.com/pub/a/linux/2002/01/31/CVS.html
