InterviewPro Enhances Advertisement Service 
 

Bronx, NY (July 22, 2004) – InterviewPro, a leading provider of career information, announced today the enhancement of its advertisement service, in which employers can describe their products or services to prospective employees. 

The previous InterviewPro advertisement service led users to an advertiser’s website when the users clicked the advertisement message on InterviewPro’s website. With the previous service, advertisers had to maintain their own websites in order to display detailed information about their products or services.

With the enhanced advertisement service, advertisers can choose either to direct users to descriptions on their own websites or to descriptions on InterviewPro’s website. Thus, this extra benefit is available even to advertisers who do not have their own websites.

"We are very pleased to enhance our advertisement service while the overall economy is booming,” said Marianna Fu, RunningSoft Vice President of Services. “Thousands of users have registered with InterviewPro. InterviewPro gets hundreds of hits from unregistered visitors every day. With our InterviewPro advertisement service, you may attract thousands of job seekers to your company." 

Availability

InterviewPro advertisement service is available at http://www.interviewpro.com/com/ with an unbeatable affordable price.

About InterviewPro

InterviewPro is a subdivision of RunningSoft; its goal is to help people pass job interviews. InterviewPro won the Quintessential Careers Site Award for its usefulness in helping many job seekers. InterviewPro can be accessed at http://www.interviewpro.com

About RunningSoft Inc.

Founded in 2004, RunningSoft is a privately held company headquartered in the Bronx, NY. Email to release at runningsoft.com URL: http://www.runningsoft.com
