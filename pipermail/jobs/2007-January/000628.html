Schematic, one of the nation's leading interactive agencies, is seeking 
a world-class Senior Software Developer to join our New York PHP team.

Senior Software Developers lead and work with project teams to 
architect, design, develop, test, document and deploy web and other 
applications. Responsibilities also include producing high quality code 
and documentation, creating timelines, providing technical expertise and 
feedback, evaluating new technologies, mentoring junior developers, 
maintaining legacy applications, and clearly communicating technical 
ideas to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Ideal candidates will have a minimum of five years experience creating 
large-scale web applications with PHP and MySQL, Oracle, or PostgreSQL. 
Experience with PHP 5 and object-oriented PHP is required, and you must 
be able to architect complex applications, write well-structured 
classes, and have a solid working knowledge of inheritance, interfaces 
and design patterns.

You must also be comfortable working with Apache, the Unix command line, 
and in team-based production scenarios, using CVS or Subversion and bug 
reporting tools such as Mantis. An excellent grasp of SQL is a must, and 
you should have experience administering relational databases. Pluses 
include development experience using other programming languages and 
experience with data modeling, Oracle, PL/SQL, scalability techniques, 
UML, unit testing, release management, e-commerce, XML, SOAP, and Flash.

Applicants should email resumes and a cover letter to 
techjobsny at schematic.com. This position is full-time/permanent, on-site 
in our New York office.
