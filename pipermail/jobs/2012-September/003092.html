Do you love helping people? Want an opportunity to work with some of the
best coaches, authors, speakers, and teachers? Are you passionate about
helping people connect and collaborate?

If so, we’d love for you to join our small and growing team at Cojourneo.

OUR MISSION AND VALUES
At Cojourneo, nothing is more important than our mission and values. They
guide everything we do, build, and dream. They are not just words on a
page. They are the soul and lifeblood of our company.
http://cojourneo.com/about/mission-values

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES
Develop and extend every part of our website platform.
Dream and plan implementation of new product features that help people
connect and collaborate.
Maintain and extend our Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure for
continued growth.
Have fun!

REQUIREMENTS
Alignment and commitment to our mission and values.
Strong knowledge of the LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP).
Experience with version control (e.g. Git).
Willingness to work virtually.
Desire and flexibility to work in an early-stage and growing startup.
Sense of humor and openness to making mistakes preferred.

COMPENSATION
Competitive pay, options, flexible work hours, unlimited vacations, and
feeling great about making the world a better place.

HOW TO APPLY
Send us an email at jobs at cojourneo.com telling us:

Why you’d love to work with us.
Why you think you're a good fit for this position.
Which Cojourneo value you are most passionate about and why.
A favorite TED (or other online) video, if you have one.
Any links to your life on the web (e.g. Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub, blog),
if you'd like to share.
Anything else you’d like us to have or know. Resumes are not required.

And, remember, we're human too. So, try to have fun and just be yourself.
:-)
