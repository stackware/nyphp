*The Christian Post* is currently accepting applications for *Project

*Description*

* *

We are seeking a *Project Manager* who is responsible for planning,
executing, and finalizing projects within strict deadlines and budget.
The *Project
Manager* will be responsible for acquiring resources and coordinating the
efforts of team members and third-party contractors or consultants in order
to deliver projects according to plan. The *Project Manager* *must be
well-versed in understanding the basics of website development in terms of
programming and designing.*





*Duties*

-Define the project’s objectives and oversee quality control throughout its
life cycle.

-Create and executes project work plans and revises as appropriate to meet
changing needs and requirements.

-Identify resources needed and assigns individual responsibilities.

-Manage day-to-day operational aspects of a project and scope.

-Review deliverables prepared by team before passing to client.

-Prepare for engagement reviews and quality assurance procedures.

-Minimize our exposure and risk on project.

-Ensure project documents are complete, current, and stored appropriately.

- *Must agree with CP's Statement of Faith* (
http://www.christianpost.com/aboutus/#statement)

indicate); e-newsletter and mass-mailing management.
