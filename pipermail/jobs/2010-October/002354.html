COMPANY DESCRIPTION



Backed by angel investors, we've spent the past two years innovating a product that is focused on powering personalized, authentic word of mouth marketing for retailers and brands.  This is an exciting time to join our company, as we are about to launch our product with a global retailer. 



Our social commerce technology combines the viral distribution benefits of a share utility with more influence power than anonymous reviews, and it works online and with any mobile device. Our technology enables customer conversations that help unify a customer's shopping experience and influence customer choices at every point along the purchase decision path.
 



About the Senior PHP Developer role: 
We're looking for a detail oriented, senior-level developer with extensive experience writing PHP code for large-scale, web-based applications. You will play a key role in helping lead our development efforts and building our application portfolio. You must be flexible and able to thrive within a dynamic, fast-paced environment.




Skills required: 
- 5+ years experience with PHP 
- OOP 
- Experience with an MVC framework (preferably Symfony) 
- Extensive experience with source control and version control tools (preferably Subversion) 
- Thorough understanding of Social Networking and Web 2.0 technologies 
- Extensive experience building and working with internal and external API's 
- Strong experience with MySQL 
- Ability to work in a small team and build consensus with colleagues 

Not required, but preferred: 
- Experience with JSON and Ajax 
- Knowledge of unix system administration 
- Experience working with API's from 3rd parties such as Facebook and MySpace 
- Experience with SMS technologies
