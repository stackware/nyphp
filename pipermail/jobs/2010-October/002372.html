Information Systems Programmer

Under the technical 
direction of the Information Systems Development & Integration 
Manager, the Information Systems Programmer/Analyst will participate in 
developing, integrating, implementing, and administrating a range of 
custom-built and packaged information systems.  
 
He/she will work as 
part of a team to modify, develop, and support systems to serve NYBG’s 
proprietary business and information need and participate in system 
analysis and planning activities.  He/she will 
code, modify, and test applications using a variety of technologies and 
environments, such as; LAMP, XML, XHTML, Delphi, JavaScript, 
post-Relational, SQL, and webs services.  The 
Information Systems Programmer/Analyst will also implement and test new 
database system developments and integrations with existing information 
systems, document system designs, develop training material, and deliver
 training.  He/she will train system and network 
administrators to respond to Helpdesk and support tasks for these 
software packages and customized systems, self-manage a continuous 
learning cycle to incorporate new skills, and perform any other relevant
 duties assigned.  
 
Requirements:
 
·         Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in an information technology development related field.
·         5
 years of experience in software or system development, at least 3 years
 of which is specific to SQL-driven web enabled processes, including 
extensive modification of legacy systems.
·         Knowledge of business system operations and support (i.e. retail, financial, CRM, e-commerce, etc).
·         Demonstrated ability to quickly learn and apply specifics of new business systems.
·         Knowledge and experience in application and database administration and support.
·         Demonstrated
 ability to do advanced script and query modifications and development 
using XML, PHP, Delphi, Javascript, XHTML, Post-relational, SQL, and 
similar environments.
·         Highly
 developed customer services skills and attitude, superior communication
 skills, a personable support manner, patience, resourcefulness, 
reliability, and a commitment to excellence.  
 
Salary commensurate with experience. Excellent Benefits. Full time position.
