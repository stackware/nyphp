Hello All,

NAPC's development group is a small & agile group that focuses on code
quality, testing, analytics, and constant enhancement. We maintain six
products that are currently deployed in over 60 remote servers around the
world. We also develop and maintain systems hosted on Amazon Web Services.

Main responsibilities of the team Most NAPC back-end components are written
in PHP, Perl, and the occasional Bash script. We operate mostly in a
GNU/Linux environment with OS X clients for development. A great deal of

work with Solr, Hadoop Map Reduce, Zend Framework, Code Igniter, and other
great new-wave technologies.

is welcome as long as the code is clean and well tested.

As a software developer at NAPC, you will develop, and maintain these
systems. You will be working with dedicated and passionate professionals.
