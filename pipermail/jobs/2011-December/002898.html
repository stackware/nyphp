seeks a PHP Developer on a contract-to-hire basis.  This is a full-time

ground up.

 

COMPETITIVE SALARY AND SUPERB BENEFITS.  COMPANY HAS A GREAT TRACK RECORD
WITH DEVELOPING EMPLOYEES.

* Develop internal applications

* Enhance existing applications

* Assist Application Manager with the development and implementation of
various internal applications,(document control systems, inventory, ISO,
dispatch/ticketing)

* Continue to develop and maintain an in place ecommerce / purchasing
application.

 

Requirements:

* Minimum of 5 years experience developing web applications in a LAMP
environment

* Minimum of 2 years experience with Magento Commerce development 

* Extensive experience developing and administering MySQL databases while
utilizing best practices

* XHTML/CSS/Javascript/AJAX - jQuery experience preferred 

* Experience in developing frontend and backend modules for Magento CE 

* Experience in optimizing application layouts for mobile devices
