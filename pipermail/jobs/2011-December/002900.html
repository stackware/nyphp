Most importantly, you need to be someone who is innovative,
collaborative, inquisitive, and confident (but not cocky), able to
meet deadlines, and have a 'do whatever it takes' attitude to your
work. We have a great office jukebox, so having a good taste in music

How to Apply:

Please drop us a line at engineer at beaffinitive.com - be sure to
