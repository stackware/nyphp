=Skills=

- Several years of experience working in PHP and Zend Framework or
similar MVC frameworks
- Several years of experience working with Javascript & JQuery
- A philosophy of unit testing all code, and experience with multiple

- Experience leading a team to complete projects within a timeframe
- Experience writing efficient code for high volume sites

- Learning all aspects of the business - from design to sales to
marketing to strategy

Salary and benefits are very competitive. We're looking for the best
of the best. You also must be able to work out of our office in NYC.
