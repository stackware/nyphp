TITLE:
Sharepoint Application Developer – New Haven, CT

COMPANY:
Enterprise software company has an immediate opening for a
SHAREPOINTAPPLICATION DEVELOPER. Company develops commercial enterprise
accounting applications for browser-base and client/server deployments.

JOB SUMMARY
Develop SharePoint applications to support business requirements for an
accounting application and its integration with overall suite of
applications. Maintain existing enterprise accounting product suite and
development of new components and features of the system.

REQUIREMENTS/QUALIFICATIONS:
Experience with development of SharePoint applications under SharePoint2007
and SharePoint 2010, including WSS and MOSS.
* Knowledge of the SharePoint structure, object model, web services and
development templates.
* Knowledge of and experience with C# and Visual Studio. Experience with C++
a plus.
* Knowledge of and experience with XML and .NET Framework.
* Experience in developing enterprise, database-driven applications.
* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related discipline.
* Experience and/or knowledge of Silverlight and the WPF framework a plus.

Candidates should be prepared to discuss their specific experience, examples
of their work, and elaborate on the technologies listed as requirements for
this job. Candidates that don’t meet these requirements won’t be considered.


BENEFITS
Company provides a comprehensive benefit plan including:
* Paid Holidays
* Paid Time Off
* 401(k) Plan with Company Participation
* 100% Company Paid Employee Health, Dental, Life and Short Term Disability
Insurance
* Long Term Disability
* Dependent Care Flexible Spending Account
* Auto/Homeowner Insurance Discount Plan
* AFLAC

There is NO RELOCATION available for this position. Local candidates only.
Only candidates that meet all of the requirements will be considered.
