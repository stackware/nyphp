The New York Botanical Garden, a National Historic Landmark, and one  
of New York City’s premier cultural institutions seeks a full-time  
Technology Specialist for Digital Media.



Technology Specialist, Digital Media

Position Application Number MBD-0134



Summary of Responsibilities:



The Technology Specialist, Digital Media is responsible for creating  
and maintaining the functionality of the Garden’s Content Management  
Systems. He/She builds and implements content for the Garden’s  
websites. Trains and assist Garden departments on Content Management  
Systems.  Creates web applications for a variety of needs and builds  
email templates and websites. Ensures consistency throughout the  
Garden’s sites and performs other relevant duties as assigned.



Qualifications:



·      Bachelor’s degree in Computer Sciences or related field  
preferred.

·      At least three plus (3+) years PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS and  
JavaScript experience.

·      Should be able to code in other languages.

·      At least one plus (1+) years of Unix/Linux experience.

·      Ability to code in a text editor required.

·      Must be able to multi-task and attention to detail is key.

·      Team oriented, excellent interpersonal and communication skills.



Salary will commensurate with experience. Excellent Benefits,  
including 4 weeks vacation.



To Apply



Qualified applicants should email resume with letter of interest to:



Recruitment & Training Specialist

Position Application Number MBD-0134

Email:  HR at NYBG.ORG





The New York Botanical Garden
