Interactive Production Assistant

 

JOB SUMMARY: 

Dynamic, fast paced interactive direct marketing agency with sustained
growth and notable clientele is seeking an experienced Interactive
Production Assistant with 1-3 years hands-on online advertising and/or
Interactive Production experience.  Responsibilities include setting up
campaigns using a web-based interface and managing/tracking the flow of
data for Company's lead generation programs.  Candidates must have
strong analytical skills and an understanding of lead formatting and
delivery methods.  Technical proficiency is REQUIRED.  

 

QUALIFICATIONS: 

*	HTML - basic knowledge
*	Macromedia/Photoshop - basic knowledge
*	Excel - intermediate

*	Detail Orientated
*	Ability to Multitask
*	Excellent Communication Skills  
*	Bachelor's Degree or Equivalent
*	Understanding of form posting, interactive marketing preferred

 

Please write job title in subject line and email resume and cover letter
to jobsnyc at innovationads.com.
