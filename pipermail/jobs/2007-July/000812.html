UltraStar Entertainment: Production Engineer ( LAMP )

Ultrastar Entertainment seeks highly motivated LAMP Production Engineer. This is a key production roll for a growing company who will report directly to the Director of Product Development and Chief Information Systems Officer.

The candidate's primary objective is to field and deliver production requests in a fast paced environment. The ideal candidate will be very familiar with Content Delivery Systems, Social Networking Applications and user-generated content applications.

This is an exciting opportunity in a growing company with a solid growth path and mentorship from a strong group of senior developers.


Responsibilities:


*	Direct the execution of daily operations and production requests
*	Ownership of CMS platform including custom and 3rd party products. Working within an existing code base, extending it, and developing new applications with it.

·          Ownership and upkeep of code base repository 

*	Develop Interface / functionality enhancements to existing products
*	Manage a Jr. Developer to assist with production requests 
*	Maintain interface usability standards
*	Maintain plug-in detection standards based on minimum requirements data
*	Field escalated bug and troubleshooting requests
*	Provide support for Streaming Media


Technical Qualifications:


*	Mastery: PHP5, MySQL, XSLT, XML, CSS, JavaScript, HTML
*	Strong: AJAX, jQuery
*	Familiarity: MVC-style frameworks, OO methodologies, PHP / MySQL optimization, Code Management and version control, Linux command line, INNO DB engine, caching schemes, 
*	PLUS: experience with Java, Ruby, Jabber / XMPP, web services, mobile/sms apps, desktop apps, Simple Machines BBS

 

Professional Qualifications 

 

*	Candidate should exhibit great attention to detail and be able to work effectively on multiple projects at once

*	Candidate should have strong interpersonal, organizational, and communication skills 
*	Highly motivated, self-directed, deadline-responsive and able to work effectively under pressure
*	Excellent project management skills and ability to develop and maintain deadlines
*	Posess Vision and Adaptability
*	Desire to dig in deep and help build the core applications and production tools for the industry leader in the space

This is a full time, on site position. We offer competitive compensation including Vacation and Holiday Pay, Medical/Dental and Life.

70K COE

How to apply:

 

Please send cover letter, salary requirements and resume to jobs [at] ultrastar.com. 

 


About UltraStar: 

 

Ultrastar Entertainment LC manages the fan club initiatives for leading artists in the entertainment industry including; The Rolling Stones, David Bowie, The Who, The Police, Inxs,  Bill & Gloria Gaither, Sting, Garbage, Keith Richards, The  Red Hot Chili Peppers and more. UltraStar currently services over three million customers a month with substantial growth.

We deliver members the utmost in insider access, including newsletters, exclusives, ticket pre-sales and packages, merchandise and community features that give fans the tools to communicate with each other.
