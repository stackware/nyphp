We are a small, well funded, rapidly growing startup working in rich  
media and advertising. We're growing rapidly, and are looking to hire  
smart, fun, and funky people who get things done.

We're looking for a good senior level php/mysql developer and project  
manager to help us create, extend, and maintain robust, scalable web  
applications.

Qualified candidates will have at least 3-4 years of industry  
experience managing web and interactive media development projects.  
Knowledge of PHP, PERL HTML, Javascript, MySQL, etc. are desired as  
is any general experience with a wide range of web development  
technologies and frameworks. Previous industry experience in Digital  
Media, Web Development/Design, or Interactive Media is ideal.  
Candidates must have a Bachelors Degree.

The Senior Developer/Project Manager will be responsible for  
requirements gathering and analysis, managing the entire project life  
cycle, design, documentation, scheduling, and resource allocation.  
Candidates must demonstrate the ability to manage multiple projects  
simultaneously and have the ability to multi-task in a fast-paced  
environment. Applicants must have excellent verbal and written  
communication skills.

DUTIES:
* Participates in a group of (initially) four to six programmers
* Participates in IT Department senior management coordinating with  
CIO and other group managers to achieve business goals
* Programs in PHP and some PERL to maintain existing systems such as  
CMS, Ad Server, Statistics reporting/logging systems, and client  
reporting tools.
* Responds to new feature requests maintaining key systems in  
accordance with long-range software development roadmaps under strict  
revision control
* Assists with maintenance of several large networks of GNU/Linux- 
based application, database, and development servers based on open- 
source software
* Updates job knowledge by keeping abreast of industry developments,  
participating in educational opportunities, reading professional  
publications, and maintaining personal networks
* Documents software and systems


REQUIRED SKILLS:

* PHP
* Expertise in configuration and maintenance of MySQL
* Perl
* Expertise in configuration and maintenance of Apache web server
* Significant Experience managing teams of developers.
* Experience managing offsite development teams.
* HTML/CSS (hand-coding in dynamic web programming applications only!)
* XML
* Experience with other scripting and programming languages (Bash,  
Ruby, Python, C, etc.) a plus!
* Proficiency in *NIX command line and with common *NIX tools
* Usage of Subversion/CVS in a complex development environment

* Excellent verbal and written communication skills
* Superior time management skills
* Software development cycle fundamentals
* Object-oriented design (OOD) experience
* Software debugging

For more info about what we do, check out our B2B site: http://nabbr.biz
Please send wage requirements, resume, and code samples.
Competitive benefits packages will be available, as well as possible  
equity positions for qualified candidates.
This is a full time, onsite position. Please, no recruiters,  
freelance dev shops from Ulaanbaatar, or telecommuters.
