Position Requirements 

Candidates must have all of the following experience: 
* Financial Services background ideal but not required 
* Experience in developing applications in an object-oriented manner,

* 5+ years of solid PHP experience

programming experience

* Experience working under UNIX servers using Apache.
* Experience with structured code libraries, frameworks, and open source
projects
* Thorough knowledge of HTML, CSS and Javascript (AJAX a plus)

* MySQL experience 
* Working knowledge of C, C++, Java, ASP.NET, C# or other OO language a
big plus.
* Excellent verbal and written communication skills. 
* Ability to react quickly to new demands in an ever-changing dynamic
business environment. 
* Sound Analytical Skills. 
* Ability to work under pressure like a 'swat' team, producing results
in a faster turnaround mode.

using, technology solutions to solve business or organizational problems
and not compromising on the architectural direction and vision. 
* Ability to work well in a fast pace environment, be flexible and
adaptable in a fast changing environment. 
* Must have a positive and a can do attitude. 
* Must be a self-motivator and be able to work with minimal direction.
