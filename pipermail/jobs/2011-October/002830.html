		Job Description

		
			
				
					Category: Web Development
CollegeHumor is seeking a Temporary Flash Developer for a 3-5 month period.
 You will work with our small talented team of developers on a number of
 large high profile websites which include: CollegeHumor, 
TodaysBIGthing, SportsPickle, Dorkly as well as contribute to a number 
of internal tools and services. Collectively our sites reach over 12 
million users every month.
  
 Overview of Role:

 CollegeHumor is seeking an ambitious, well-rounded Flash Developer to 
temporarily take over development and maintenance of our homegrown video
 player.  You need to think like a user, be passionate about coding and 
excited to build and iterate features that impact millions of people. 
  

 This role will be responsible for everything Video and Flash on 
CollegeHumor and its sister sites while our current Flash developer is 
on leave. The candidate will train with our Flash developer to get up to
 speed with our video player.  The candidate will be managing the video 
players for all sites, adding cutting-edge features into the Flash 
codebase, and using JavaScript to deeply interact with the page around 
it. This role will work with our ad operations team to integrate ad and 
stat systems into the framework.
  
 The candidate must enjoy working in a fast paced environment with small agile development teams.
  
 Requirements:  Fluent in Actionscript 3 and OO programming. Experience developing an online video player or an online video application of comparable complexity. Experience integrating Flash components with both AS3 and JS-based APIs. Knowledge of MVC or related code frameworks and experience with a moderately sized codebase. Ability to convert Photoshop mockups into pixel-precise and optimized Flash graphics as necessary. Experience working with plugins or external APIs. Familiarity with HTML, CSS, Javascript (PHP a plus).  
 How To Apply: 
 This position is on-site at our NYC office in Chelsea.
  
 To be considered, please send cover letter, resume, and coding samples.
Apply Here:  http://www.apply-for-job.net/c/jobclick.cfm?site=12168&job=8683131
