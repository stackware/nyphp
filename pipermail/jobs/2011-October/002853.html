Sr. Python/Django Developer
$90,000 base

Client is an open source products and services company building collaboration centric application for our clients. Our primary focus is building custom Collaborative Dashboards, Business-Social Media and Mobile applications on cloud platforms.  We have several products that leverage Django/Python framework. Our clients range from IBM, major Investment firms to early stage startups.

Our team has a strong passion for cutting edge technologies. At our core, we are a technology company. We have been at the forefront of the recent technology shifts in the last decade - open source, web 2.0, and cloud computing. We've been leveraging web 2.0 technologies and cloud computing since its early days. As mobile technologies reaches maturity, ubiquitous access is becoming more of a reality and is increasingly becoming part of our solution set.

Responsibilities:
We are looking for individuals who can work in a dynamic team environment and help craft and build innovative solutions for our clients. We are looking for programmers who can help us extend our product set as we add more features.

  *   Build software components that comply with the application architecture and detailed design
  *   Work with project managers and/or clients to scope work, produce estimates and to help define deliverables and timelines
  *   Select technologies, recommend architectures and create technical design based on client requirements

Requirements:

  *   At least three years' experience developing web application in Django/Python
  *   Four to seven years' experience developing database-driven, web applications
  *   Solid experience with front end development (HTML, JavaScript, CSS, AJAX) a must
  *   Proficiency across the range of J2EE technologies and frameworks like Struts, Spring is a solid plus
  *   Strong Object Oriented Design and experience developing high-performance systems and web sites (caching, performance, messaging, serialization, etc.)
  *   Knowledge of RDBMS, database design. Worked with at least one of the following: Oracle, Sybase, MySQL, or PostgreSQL
  *   Experience working in a fast changing environment with tight deadlines
  *   Ability to clearly communicate ideas and technical decisions



Most of our clients/projects are based in New York and New Jersey. However, as a consultant, travel is required as needed.
