The Sr. PHP Developer, reporting to the VP of Technology, will build commercial, enterprise web applications to create breakthrough opportunities for innovative software solutions in the healthcare field. This person will use PHP and mySQL in a LAMP production environment to build and enhance robust software applications. The ideal candidate will work to drive LanguageMate's products from inception to commercialization and be responsible for delivering the highest quality, successful, meaningful software products that make a positive impact throughout the healthcare continuum.
The Sr. PHP Developer will be responsible for using proven industry standard software development methodologies and processes to build, enhance and manage a technical product in our innovative portfolio of healthcare informatics tools. The initial product offerings are very strong, and have been tested and approved by a list of major initial customers. This is a great opportunity for an experienced and dynamic professional to develop new applications for an innovative solution in a high-growth sector.

Ideally the Sr. PHP Developer will have a good start-up track record and be a natural and innovative team member within the health care technology sector, preferably working to address the issues of underserved communities. This person must be highly motivated, entrepreneurial, and interested in the social aspect of the business.
Responsibilities


 *            Design, develop, implement, and maintain web software applications
 *            Review business requirements and functional specifications providing analysis and feedback
 *            Manage a technical product and participate in project estimation and goal setting
 *            Deliver projects built to scope, according to agreed upon goals
 *            Create and perform unit and integration tests
 *            Create and maintain code documentation
 *            Debug and troubleshoot software defects

 Qualifications


 *            5+ years professional experience in object oriented PHP5 and mySQL application development
 *            Expertise building applications using PHP5, mySQL, SQL, MVC, and AJAX
 *            Solid understanding of object oriented programming concepts and principles a must
 *            Significant experience building Zend Framework applications
 *            In-depth knowledge of software engineering processes
 *            At least a BS in computer science or related discipline, more advanced technical degree preferred
Compensation
Salary is competitive and commensurate with experience. The company offers excellent health insurance, 401(k), pre-tax transit benefits, and education benefits.

Company
LanguageMate(r) is a leader in the development of cutting edge informatics solutions that address challenges throughout the care delivery continuum, including patient-provider communication, workflow management, and outreach to diverse populations. Operational constraints in clinical practice and health education contribute to many of the medical errors, unnecessary costs, and inadequate preventive care practices that result in adverse health outcomes and persistent inefficiency in the healthcare system. By delivering innovative solutions, we support all stakeholders with tools that facilitate community engagement, streamline provider and administrative workloads, and elevate the quality of patient care. Since 2004, the National Institutes of Health has awarded our team over a dozen Small Business Innovation Research grants to develop a portfolio of highly innovative tools and a growing body of rigorous scientific evidence to demonstrate their efficacy, adoptability, and long-term sustainability. Our team consists of clinicians, public health researchers, educators, linguists, and anthropologists working closely with professionals in information technology, interactive design, multimedia production, and business management. We take tremendous pride in our work, which enables patients and service providers to transcend the barriers of language, culture, technology, and geography.
