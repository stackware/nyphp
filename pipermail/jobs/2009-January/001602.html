Position Description:

What are you passionate about? Affinitive (www.beaffinitive.com) is  
seeking an experienced Lead/Senior Software Engineer to manage and  
contribute to Affintive's day-to-day, social media-driven technology- 
related operations. We've got an awesome work environment full of  
creative marketers and technologists, and are looking for someone who  
can thrive within our casual, yet hard-working company culture.

This is a senior-level role with a small development team, so the  
ability to operate as both a leader and as an individual contributor  
is a must. Reporting to the company President, you will act as a  
liaison between the development team and marketing/project managers.  
You will be responsible for ensuring the technology team meets  
deadlines, stays on budget and continues to innovate.

You must be both a left- and right- brained thinker, wearing a  
"startup" hat in terms of being an agile, "ninja" developer  
responsible for evolving and innovating Affinitive's technologies, and  
an "agency" hat in terms of thriving in a fast-paced, marketing-based  
agency environment where solutions are applied in custom, creative  
ways for clients across industry verticals (as well as internally).  
You will act both as a mentor to our engineering team and as a client- 
facing technician able to articulate very detailed issues to non- 
technical people.

Although our solutions are proprietary, we embrace and leverage open- 
source technologies so you must possess expertise across the entire  
LAMP stack, and have proven experience developing robust, highly  
scalable, Web-based platforms (preferably multi-tenant, SaaS-based  
solutions related to social media, online communities, or CRM).

Now for the details...

Responsibilities:

• Provide ongoing technical leadership and expertise on system  
architecture and planning, application development and deployment/ 
integration, security, and testing of all technology initiatives
• Manage team of developers while acting as a lead, who develops and  
take ownership of key areas of application functionality
• Gather requirements/scope for initiatives and develop project plans,  
working with project management and account teams to provide budgeting  
and resource planning on projects and ensuring projects stay on  
schedule and on budget
• Audit existing processes and technology and establish software  
engineering best practices and development guidelines to consistently  
deliver quality software
• Provide innovation with regards to advancing Affinitive's technology  
offerings
• Provide individual mentoring and lead code reviews

Qualifications:

• 5-8 years of experience (3+ as lead) architecting and developing  
successful, large-scale, enterprise LAMP-based applications  
(preferably multi-tenant, SaaS- solutions related to social media,  
online communities, or CRM)
• B.S. or M.S. in Computer Science or equivalent experience
• Expertise with object-oriented, MVC-based PHP frameworks (CakePHP,  
Zend), VPSes, AJAX/JavaScript frameworks (Prototype, JQuery, YUI),  
load balancing/scalability, memcached, enterprise search (Lucene/ 
Sphinx), cloud computing and storage (S3, E3), *nix administration,  
Apache/Litespeed, MySQL, template frameworks (Smarty), scalability  
(replication, sharding), server monitoring (Nagios, Cacti), SVN, unit  
testing (Selenium), bug tracking systems (Trac, Mantis), XHTML, XML/ 
RSS, CSS
• Experience with agile and test-driven development processes
• Experience building RESTful web services APIs for developers and  
integrating third-party web service APIs

Bonus Points:

• Experience integrating with CRM systems like Salesforce.com, content  
management systems (CMS), and legacy systems
• Security experience in roles/authentication/authorization, single- 
sign-on (SSO) and protocols (Facebook connect, OpenID), and cross-site  
connectivity
• Facebook/OpenSocial and iPhone application development experience
• Expertise with other scripting and object-oriented programming  
languages

Ideal Background/Profile:

• A natural technical leader with strong interpersonal skills and a  
collaborative approach to problem-solving
• Customer-focused architect who understands the "big picture"  
objectives of solutions that are developed as they relate to business  
objectives
• Contributor to one or more open source projects
• Ability to multitask and respond to dynamically changing priorities
• Ability to think creatively and solve problems
• Excellent oral and written communication skills
• Hard working, excellent attention to detail, and fun to work with!

Compensation and Benefits:

• Competitive salary and performance based bonus structure,  
commensurate with experience
• Great benefits including full medical and dental coverage, 401(k),  
and Flexible Spending Account (FSA)
