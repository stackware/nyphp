Unype, Inc. is a well-funded location-based social-networking startup
based in New York City. We have iPhone, Android, Blackberry and
Windows Mobile applications and social-network applications in
Facebook, MySpace, Hi5, Orkut, Bebo, Friendster and Ning with hundreds
of thousands of users.

Our culture is based on Silicon Valley where we cut our teeth: fun,

Only local candidates will be considered.
And no third-parties or recruiters, please.

Requirements:
* Expert knowledge of PHP and a PHP-Caching framework
* Expert knowledge of MySql, replication with multiple slaves,
backup/restore procedures
* Expert knowledge of Linux, Apache
* Experience with MemCached
* Experience with load-balancing
* Experience with Amazon EC2, S3 and Persistent Storage
* Experience with highly-scalable web services
