Job Description:

As a User Interface Support Developer you will play a key role in the
production of Saber Seven Technologies. Direct responsibilities will
include the instantiation and maintenance of clean, browserindependent
dynamic interfaces used for both public and internal web
systems. This position will also assist in documenting data
requirements for web interfaces based on preexisting specifications
created in conjunction with the Senior Interactive Producer and Saber
Seven's Information Architecture team, specifying the data exchange
format with other constituents, and providing the implementation of
content presentation software.

As a User Interface Support Developer your responsibilities may also
include the documentation and implementation of interfaces with
internal services. You will work closely with the other members of the
Technology Team to ensure that data delivery features will be
compliant with our standards, and leverage existing code resources
whenever possible.

Functional Areas:
Web Interface development experience
• PHP Templating Systems such as SMARTY
* Javascript
* AJAX
* Flash Remoting

Other desired attributes:
* Demonstrated leadership and personnel management skills as well
as excellent proficiency in all communications methods
* Strong relationship and interpersonal skills
* Strong verbal and written communication skills (cross-functionally
and interdepartmentally)
* Strong analytical, organizational, prioritization and time management
skills
* Relevant industry experience preferred
* Multi-national experience a plus
* Experience in forming strategic partnerships with functional
technology and business executives preferred

To apply, please forward you resume to: marc.isikoff at saberseveninc.com
