PHP/MySQL Software Engineer
Duration: Contract on-site, 40 hours/week
Location: New York City (Manhattan)

What are you passionate about? At Affinitive (www.beaffinitive.com), we are

Description:

Affinitive is seeking a skilled PHP/MySQL Software Engineer to join our
development team and work on fun and innovative technology projects in the
word of mouth/viral marketing space. Although this will initially be a
contract position, there is potential for it to transition into a full-time
one. This position will require that you work on-site at our Manhattan-based
office (no telecommuters please).

Duties and Responsibilities:

* Work with our development team to implement new functionality and features
into our proprietary technology platforms. You may be asked to take the lead
role on certain projects.
* Manage testing and deployment of builds and updates.
* Develop web-based administrative and reporting tools based on functional
specs.
* Work with design staff to ensure a high level of usability.
* Read user requirement documents and assist in writing system
specifications.
* Perform ongoing support and some system administration.
* Meet aggressive deadlines and handle multiple tasks.

Qualifications:

* BS in Computer Science (or related) field.
* Expertise (2+ years) of object-oriented application development experience
using LAMP (Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP).
* Expertise (2+ years) in database schema design, programming, and
performance tuning, specifically using MySQL.
* Experience (2+ years) with XHTML, XML/RSS, JavaScript, CSS, Smarty, and
CVS for version control.
* AJAX experience is a big plus.
* Great taste in music is a HUGE plus :)
* Ability to think creatively and solve problems.
* Excellent oral and written communication skills.
* Be a hard worker, have attention to detail, and be fun to work with!

How to apply:

Please email a resume, cover letter, and rates (important!) with the subject
line reading "PHP/MySQL Software Engineer" to jobs at beaffinitive.com. In your
cover letter, please tell us a little bit about yourself, detailing any
relevant technology/marketing experience. You may be asked to provide some
code samples. No recruiters or off-site contractors please.
