PHP Managers/Architects, NYC | 80-110k  

My client's core technology is developed in a C/Unix environment. The UI and reporting tool is developed in PHP. Due to growth and increased development needs, they are seeking experienced Development Managers/Architects to join our Technology group.

These positions have responsibility for all development and support of our ad-server technology. They have hands-on coding responsibilities, as well as leadership responsibilities, requiring a strong background in computer science, and ownership of the full project life cycle.

Primary Responsibilities include:

• Responsible for owning a large, critical application 
• Provide experience leading all phases of the software development lifecycle 
• Provide Project Management experience (budget planning, work planning, risk analysis, etc.) 
• Provide programming expertise in optimization techniques and algorithms 
• Provide leadership to other developers 
• Build a strong and effective development group 

Qualifications include:

• BA/BS in Computer Science, related degree, or comparable experience 
• 5+ years of professional software development experience 
• Technical expertise should include C, Java, PHP, MySQL 
• Solid understanding of OO programming concepts 
• Serious professional experience developing large, complex systems 
• Experience with optimization techniques and algorithms 
• Experience with high-volume, rapid transaction processing systems is required 
• Strong interpersonal skills 

To be considered for this position, candidates:

MUST live in the tri-state (NY, NJ, CT) area. 
MUST work on-site at clients NYC location. 
MUST be auth to work in the USA 

These are full time, salaried, on-site positions in New York City.  There is NO chance of them hiring a telecommuter or a team of 100 developers from India.

Please submit your resume and a paragraph highlighting your relevant experience as it pertains to this job to beau at open-source-staffing.com
