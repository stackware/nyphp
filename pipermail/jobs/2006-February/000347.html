WEB PROGRAMMER & SYSTEMS ADMINISTRATOR

Company information:

The New York Post is a mass circulation newspaper in tabloid format that
has enjoyed unprecedented circulation growth making it America's fastest
growing newspaper. Also America's oldest continuously published daily
newspaper, The Post recently celebrated its 200th anniversary since
Alexander Hamilton founded it. Home of powerful brands like PageSix, and
The Best Sports in Town, the Post is one of the top New York newspapers
for news, sports, entertainment and much more. The Post is part of the
News Corporation family.

The New York Post Online Edition seeks a mid-level programmer to join
our day staff in the Midtown New York City offices. We are looking for a
responsible, self-directed individual with an enthusiasm for open source
software technologies and demonstrated experience with them in an online
environment.

Job specifications:

* This is a permanent, full-time, onsite position. Please no freelance
inquires and no placement services!
* This position requires candidate to have excellent writing and
communication skills.
* Candidate must be able to work on several projects at a time
* This position reports to the Director of the Online Edition.
* Salary range: up to $70,000 depending on experience, skill level

Skill set includes:

* Familiarity with HTML, CSS, Javascript and web design technologies
* Minimum 2-3 years demonstrated experience with PHP
* Beginning to moderate experience with Perl in both CGI and standalone
environments
* Demonstrated problem solving and solution building skills
* Demonstrated experience with open source databases and database
programming (experience with database planning and architecture a plus,
experience with PostgreSQL also a plus)
* Demonstrated administrative experience with Linux or BSD operating systems
* Familiarity with Apache installation, configuration and maintenance is
preferred
* Demonstrated ability to be flexible and adaptable to changing
requirements in a continuously evolving environment
* Ability to deliver high standards while working within tight deadlines
in a pressurized environment
* Familiarity with working in a Media or fast paced environment

Responsibilities:

* Will be expected to work with online team to maintain existing online
systems
* Must manage and develop various online projects, including the
creation and automation of complex statistical reporting systems,
dynamic sections of the website, e-commerce systems, and database
replication
* Assist Manager in maintenance and development of production database
and web servers
* Maintain and develop website's staging environment
* Perform website Q/A and apply fixes as needed

To apply, please send resume and cover letter including compensation
requirements to: hjohnson at nypost.com.

Heather Johnson
Director, Online Edition
New York Post
hjohnson at nypost.com
