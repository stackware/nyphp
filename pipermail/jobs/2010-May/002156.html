Web Developer


LimeWire, maker of the famous P2P file-sharing program and digital music
store, seeks an exceptional WEB DEVELOPER.  The ideal candidate will be
innovative, self-motivated, a quick study, and willing to develop new
skills while constantly improving existing abilities. In this position,
you will work closely with teammates to build and maintain (among other
things) LimeWire.com, a site receiving millions of unique visitors per
day. This is a mid to senior-level development position. 


Responsibilities 


*	Work alongside talented developers to enhance and maintain
LAMP-based production site (LimeWire.com) 
*	Participate in (and sometimes lead) the technical planning,
architecture and development of new features and systems 
*	Improve analytics (WebTrends, Google Analytics, homegrown) 
*	Enhance and maintain system scripts using PHP and Perl 
*	Mine massive databases and logs to discover meaningful information
and trends (MySQL, grep, sed, awk, etc.) 
*	Manage installations of Drupal, WordPress, and MediaWiki 

 


Requirements 


*	Primary focus: PHP, Drupal, MySQL, Apache, Linux 
*	Broad range of software development experience 
*	Ability and desire to work in high-traffic production environment 
*	Experience developing structured PHP applications and systems 
*	Experience with OO PHP development using frameworks and patterns 
*	Strong database skills (theory, architecture, development,
optimization) 
*	Experience working with open source technologies 
*	Ability to use the command line efficiently 

 


To Apply 


Please send resumes to bgormsen at limewire.com 


 


More About The Company 


LimeWire is the maker of the most popular file-sharing software in the
world. 

LimeWire prides itself on its great products, one of the most engaged user
bases in the world, and an incredible team of talent. Since launching the
LimeWire application in 2000, we have continuously sought ways to empower
our users. Recently, we have been busy with the development of the
LimeWire Store and creating more ways for Lime Wire's peer-to-peer
community to connect. 

LimeWire is also active in the open source community. The LimeWire
application is released under the GNU General Public License, and LimeWire
developers have engaged in various open source projects such as Spring,
HttpClient, Glazed Lists, libTorrent, Wicket, and Hazelcast to name a few.


We are always determined to do much more and seek gifted and inspired
people to join our team. We offer competitive salaries, excellent
benefits, and great perks including 5 weeks of paid time off, free
lunches, snacks, and drinks, tickets to cultural events, company-sponsored
outings, fabulous rooftop parties, and coworkers who are also great
friends. Located in the SoHo neighborhood of Manhattan, we foster a happy,
healthy work environment where a free exchange of ideas is encouraged.
Ideal candidates thrive in our do-it-yourself, entrepreneurial atmosphere.


Interested in Lime Wire? We look forward to hearing from you. 

Please send resumes to bgormsen at limewire.com
