About us:

We started out in 2001 wanting to make bibliographies really easy. We
created EasyBib.com, which today have evolved research platform to create
citations, take notes, and discover new information. Over 1 in every 2
students in the US uses EasyBib.

We do more than EasyBib. ResearchReady is our online learning platform that
teaches and assess research skills. Along the way, we became very good at
building technologies and strategies to monetize digital ads. Our
StudyBreakMedia team optimizes ad revenue for our properties and partner
properties.

We’re most excited about our next product (in beta mode), which aims to
make online course creation simple, beautiful, and easy.

We are listed as 971 in Inc Magazines 5000 fastest growing private
companies in the US. And we’ve been feature in publication such as The Next
Web, Huffington Post, US News & World Report, Mashable, Venturebeat, ZDNet,
Booklist, The Library Journal, and the Chicago Tribune.

We’re looking for all types of developers.

   -

   -

   -

   -

   -

   -

   -
