Systems Engineer (PHP Developer)

Interwoven is looking for a full-time application developer with
exceptional talent, an abundance of energy, and a high-degree of
self-motivation, to work with our Optimost Platform. We are the leading
provider of multivariable testing solutions for companies doing business
on the web, making this a challenging position not suitable for novices.
The ideal candidate has 5+ years of professional work experience the
areas of: 

* PHP 
* MySQL 
* JavaScript 
* HTML 
* CSS 
* XML 
* Red Hat Linux 

Our architecture is object based, so a solid understanding of object
oriented programming concepts and principles is crucial. Experience with
other languages such as Perl, C++, .NET, etc. is preferred. Excellent
written communication, organizational, and time-management skills, and a
conscientious nature are required. 

To apply, email a copy of your resume to jcomerfo at interwoven.com
