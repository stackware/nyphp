Summary

Ultra Logistics has an immediate career opportunity for an ambitious, motivated individual to join our Technology department in Fair Lawn, NJ.​  We offer a fast-paced and stimulating team environment and leverage the latest technologies to deliver quality services to our clients.​ We are currently looking for a full-time programmer with expertise in PHP & SQL (MySQL & MSSQL) to join our team. Additional qualifications include experience with JavaScript & CSS. The majority of our applications now run on Linux systems with MySQL DBs. Candidate will be part of a small team of programmers working on web based logistics software. We are a results oriented company and as such the candidate must be able to work well under pressure and meet deadlines.

Responsibilities

• Design and development of cutting edge web apps used to support our transportation industry services

• Troubleshoot and maintain existing code base

• Participate in the overall design and direction of our applications development

 

Requirements:
• Expertise in PHP & SQL
• Experience with AJAX a plus

• Excellent debugging and troubleshooting skills

• Strong communication, analytical, and teamwork skills 

• Desire to take initiative & be a self-starter

 

Additional Info

• Understanding of Web 2.​0 concepts, dynamic user interfaces - AJAX

• Experience with large databases/​commercial web sites

• Understands the fundamental concepts of OOP and MVC

 

About Ultra Logistics:
Ultra Logistics is a national 3rd party logistics provider that is looking for a qualified individual to become part of our IT team in our northern New Jersey headquarters.​ We currently provide software to many Fortune 500 & Fortune 1000 companies. 

Our employees enjoy a professional, modern, pleasant office environment and are supplied with the training and tools they need to succeed.​ We offer competitive compensation with excellent medical, vision and dental benefits.​

 

We are the direct employer, we are not an agency.​ This is a full-time position, not a contract.​
