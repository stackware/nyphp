We've developed an innovative technology that is currently in beta, which leverages social-networking technology to create a unique consumer experience that will revolutionize viral marketing.  We're looking for a detail oriented Senior PHP/MySQL developer with extensive experience building large-scale, web-based applications.



We work in a dynamic fast-paced environment, where it is crucial to adapt easily to change.  The right candidate will be self-directed, able to work well independently and as part of a small team, building and reaching consensus among peers, and leading junior team members /  consultants as needed.



REQUIREMENTS:
- 5 yrs OO PHP

- 5 yrs MySQL
- MVC framework (Symfony preferred)
- Experience with source control / version control tools (Subversion)
- Solid understanding of social networking and internet technologies
- Experience with API's (3rd parties such as Facebook and MySpace preferred)
- JSON, Ajax and SMS technologies helpful but not required

- Authorized to work in the US without sponsorship

- Local candidates only, please. Relocation is NOT available.



Compensation includes competitive base salaries plus bonus potential.  Sorry, equity is not being offered at this stage. The company is privately-held, self-funded.  Our office is located in Union Square.



For more information on the above-described fulltime, permanent opportunity please email tech at riverviewrecruiting.com.
