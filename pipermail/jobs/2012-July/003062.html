new features, maintenance and enhancements. We are an entertainment site

Site Facts: The site is built with PHP, MySQL5 Database and Custom
Framework.

Requirements
- 5+ years proficient in PHP, SQL, HTML, CSS development. Experience w/LAMP

- Knowledge and basic understanding of other languages and tools including

algorithm design.
- Keep up-to-date with the latest relevant technologies and implement web
development best practices.
- Knowledge and passion of social networks, tools, platforms and sites is
required.
- Knowledge of security based issues including cookies and encryption .
- High level understanding of internet services, such as SOAP, xmlrpc, wddx
etc.
