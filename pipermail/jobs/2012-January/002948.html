 *Fashion Technology Company seeks Senior Software Engineer*

*
*

*About LOOKBOOKS: *

*
*

LOOKBOOKS is a tech-based fashion media company providing integrated
marketing solutions in the fashion industry. Our clients are leaders in
their fields and use our software and services to represent some of the
finest talent in the world.



We are a young, financially secure, fast growing company with excellent
long-term opportunities and tremendous growth potential.



LOOKBOOKS offers employees a comprehensive array of benefits including
medical, dental and a generous bonus schedule.

* *

*
*

*Ideal Candidate:*

*
*

You are a seasoned LAMP developer with 8+ years experience developing
custom applications using MVC frameworks. An ideal candidate would be
comfortable working along side industry veterans with proven track records
for success, and should enjoy finding elegant solutions to large scale,
highly complex problems.



This position is full-time and on-site in midtown Manhattan.

*Salary is up to $160,000/year + signing bonus. (doe)*





*Responsibilities:*


   - Architect, design, code and test LAMP web applications
   - Test and enhance existing product features
   - Develop new products and features from the ground up
   - Write clean and well tested code
   - Work well in an agile team environment



*Requirements:*


   - In-depth knowledge of Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP (LAMP)
   - Advanced knowledge of Javascript, AJAX, jQuery, HTML
   - Have built applications using a MVC framework such as CakePHP, Zend,
   CodeIgniter, Symfony, etc.
   - Knowledge of source control systems (Git, Subversion)
   - Desire to work in an exciting, fast-paced, entrepreneurial, startup
   environment
   - Passion for and experience in developing great web products end-to-end
   - Ability to add your own previous experience and intuition to
   positively influence the product
   - Excellent verbal and written communication skills



*Preferred:*


   - Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science
   - Experience with developing in Ruby or Python
   - Experience with a NoSql database (redis, mongodb, couchdb, etc)
   - Experience building social networking / media websites
   - Experience with large, scalable PHP / MySQL websites
   - Interest in the fashion industry



Please submit resumes to jobs at lookbooks.com.

This is a On-Site position only.
