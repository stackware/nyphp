Responsibilities
- Converting web page designs (mockups) in to HTML templates using CSS.
- Create “themes” for Drupal based sites
- Work on variety of projects

Work Experience Requirements:
- Minimum 5-7 years of experience, with 2 years of Drupal experience (samples of applications that you have worked on are required)
- Excellent knowledge in writing standards based, semantic xHTML and CSS.
