The engineer is expected to have strong understanding of web technology

drive resolution with cross functional teams 
o Strong decision-making skills, and an understanding of where projects fit
into the overall architecture strategy

changes into opportunities. Proven performance record with demonstrated
leadership skills 
o Excellent communication, negotiation and facilitation skills are required

repositories with GIT
