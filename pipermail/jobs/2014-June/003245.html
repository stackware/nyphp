Senior PHP Developer - New York City 
- This is a full time position 
- Hired developer will work on-site in New York City 
- Salary to 150k (dependent on experience) 
- Citizenship: Required. Greed Card Ok 

My New York City client designs and builds enterprise business applications
for web, social and mobile media. They are looking to fill a full time,
salaried Senior PHP Developer position with the following experience: 

We are looking for a PHP developer who can write PHP code at a very high
level with good documentation habits. The candidate should be very
comfortable in a LAMP environment. In addition to custom PHP solutions, my
client supports a variety of PHP based frameworks that often need
supplemental code to enhance a framework's functionality. Favorable
consideration will be given to candidates with experience in Drupal and
Symfony. Magento and WordPress are also attractive. You will be a part of a
creative team that is responsible for all aspects of the ongoing software
development from the initial specification, through to developing, testing
and launching. 

Responsibilities: 

* Write "clean", well designed object oriented code 
* Produce detailed specifications 
* Troubleshoot, test and maintain the core product software and databases to
ensure strong optimization and functionality 
* Contribute in all phases of the development lifecycle 
* Follow industry best practices 
* Develop and deploy new features to facilitate related procedures and tools
if necessary 

Requirements: 

* 5+ years of software development experience in PHP 
* Understanding of open source projects like Drupal, Symfony, etc 
* Demonstrable knowledge of web technologies including HTML, CSS,
JavaScript, AJAX etc 
* Good knowledge of relational databases, version control tools and of
developing web services 
* Experience in common third-party APIs (google, facebook, ebay etc) 
* Passion for best design and coding practices and a desire to develop new
bold ideas 
* BS/MS degree in Computer Science, Engineering or a related subject 

To be considered for this amazing opportunity, please submit your resume
along with your salary requirements to dan at swift-staffing.com
