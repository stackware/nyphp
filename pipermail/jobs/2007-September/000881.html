Hello.  I would like to post the below job to the list members.  Thanks!


Position: Web Technology Developer

Company: Music Entertainment Company
Group: Digital Business Ops
Location: NYC- Midtown
Type: Permanent/Full Time

Job Summary:
The Web Technology developer is responsible for developing and maintaining several of the Company's web based applications.  These systems are core to delivery of the Company's digital content including our mobile content application and our direct to consumer mobile store, communicating regularly with mobile content engineers, mobile providers and the Company's Global Digital Business group.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities:
The Web Technology Developer responsibilities may include, but will not be limited to:

* Server side web development
* Focus on delivery of content to mobile devices
* Focus on content production applications using XSL and Object Oriented PHP
* Participate in all phases of the development cycle
* Communicate with mobile providers and media services in regards to developing new technologies
* Project manage rolling out direct to consumer technology and process internationally
* Design & develop new enhancements to cutting edge audio and video transcoding engine
* Extend and manage a highly dynamic distributed computing environment used to power direct-to-consumer applications as well as internally focused tools

Education and/or Experience:

* Server side web development experience is required
* Knowledge of Java or OO PHP is required
* Experience with XSL a strong plus
* Knowledge of mobile content delivery technologies, rudimentary digital signal processing and a love of music are added pluses
* 4+ years of web applications programming experience (Java, PHP)
* Expertise with data models and SQL
* Experience with MySQL and MySQL database tools
* Excellent problem solving, design, and communication skills
* Knowledge of Linux servers
* BREW development experience a strong plus
