Snooth, the world's largest wine review site and social network, is  
seeking an experienced PHP developer. You'll work with a smart,  
dedicated engineering team to build the next generation of the site.  
If you enjoy the challenges and opportunity of working with a startup  
with the benefits of a mature company Snooth may be for you.

Candidates should have at least 3 years of professional experience  
working with PHP and SQL on large, scalable web applications.  
Specific experience with PostgreSQL is a plus. Duties will include  
developing site features and taking part in high level software  
architecture projects. Experience working with Perl and Java are  
helpful but not a requirement.

Candidates will be encouraged to implement a project from inception  
to launch quickly and efficiently with attention to the application-  
wide details of the implementation. Candidate will report to the CTO.

Qualifications/Requirements

* 3+ years of PHP / SQL experience
* Team player
* Detail-oriented, willing to get your hands dirty and ability to  
move fast while making minimal mistakes
* Demonstrated capacity to both plan and build large scalable web  
applications
* PostgreSQL experience a plus
* Perl and Java experience a plus, but not a requirement
* CS degree a plus

Snooth's team is smart, ambitious, and passionate; the environment is  
relaxed and a lot of fun despite lots of tight deadlines. We are  
focused on quick, quality completion of projects and excited by what  
we do. If you like wine, that's always a plus.

If you fit the profile above, email us at jobs at snooth.com. Your cover  
letter must include salary requirements and what inspired you to  
apply for a position at Snooth. Resumes without cover letters will  
NOT be considered.

Please reference the job title in your subject line.

About Snooth:
Snooth is revolutionizing the way people choose wine. We're the  
world's largest and fastest growing wine review site and social  
network. We're a nimble, fast growing, New York based, venture  
capital backed company and a great place for smart, hard working  
people to make a difference and help us change the wine industry.

All positions are full time, unless stated, based in New York and  
come with comprehensive benefits, belying our startup status.
