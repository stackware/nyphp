Sr. PHP Developer/Architect ($115-140k base maybe more)

Financial Firm - Downtown NYC

It is critical that the candidate be a well rounded developer who has
experience developing for projects that have a significant level of
complexity but at the same time have a thorough understanding of the
full-lifecycle of an Enterprise-level web development project. This project
is considered amongst the top initiatives

 

Responsibilities

*	Developing code for the targeted environment as well as
participating in architectural discussions that are relevant to their domain
of expertise or responsibility.
*	Proposing and documenting software design, analyzing requirements,
and presenting implementation options.
*	Understanding the full software development lifecycle (SDLC).
*	Embracing development best practices, and communicating clearly and
effectively with peers, users, testers, and supervisors.
*	Hands on, approach analyzing existing code, writing new code,
testing, debugging and providing production support.


Experience and Skills:


*	A minimum of 7 years of experience designing, implementing,
developing and delivering advanced PHP web applications
*	Zend Framework experience (although other MVC frameworks like
Symfony & CakePHP are relevant)
*	Past experience as a developer on an enterprise project that used
LAMP technologies, with a demonstrated ability to articulate the
architectural solution and the solutions applied.
*	Thorough understanding of MVC design patterns with the ability to
articulate specific solutions through past experiences
*	Ability to develop for scalability including understanding advanced
database issues, clustering, and caching.
*	Hands-on ability with MySql, including advanced database design,
query generation, performance tuning and profiling in high volume
environment
*	Working knowledge with front-end technologies, especially jQuery,
Ajax, and JSON
*	Understanding and dedication to software development "best
practices" including familiarity with agile development methodologies
*	Strong experience with design-for-testability and unit-tests
*	Version control experience with Subversion (or Git)
*	Enough understanding of Apache Web Server to maintain a development
environment and to support production issues
*	Excellent problem solving and communication skills both spoken and
written.
*	Proven ability to work in concert with other developers and teams,
but also possess the ability to work independently to create solutions.
*	BS/BA in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or a related
field.
*	Financial industry (fixed income) experience preferred but not
required.     

Zend Certification is a major plus
