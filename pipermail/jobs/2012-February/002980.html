PHP Lead Position

$100-120K + equity

My client is an Internet Marketing & E-commerce driven Company on Long
Island (Nassau County) that is actively seeking a PHP Developer with strong

*	Programming & web development

*	Significant independent web development experience necessary

functionality

*	Programming experience: PHP, MySQL, Javascript, HTML5, CSS, JQuerry

*	A strong online portfolio, demonstrating elegant and clean

*	Strong problem solving skills

.         Involved in full life cycle development process

.         Responsible for web content development by taking creative assets

.         Web-site maintenance

.         Responsible for creating, publishing and maintaining online
