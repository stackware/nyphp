Please Respond to jobs at berkleynetworks.com

Senior Applications Developer / Berkley Networks

About our company:

Berkley Networks Corporation is a venture focused on bringing a new  
utility for retailers to market.  Our technology is a new easy to  
deploy shopping utility that enable consumers to bookmark and add to a  
wish list products from retailers ecommerce sites, print  
advertisements, or even while shopping in stores.

The Senior Applications Developer role:

The Sr. Developer will play a key role in continuing design,  
development, deployment and operations of company’s products and  
services in a consistent and efficient manner.

The primary responsibility will be providing a key input into the  
technical design, execution of development and support of our  
platform.  We require our team members to take ownership of the design  
and development efforts.

We expect our Senior Developer to use a holistic approach when  
executing on our technical opportunities deploying solutions, which  
take into account architecture, technology selection, system  
integration, database design and security implementation.

Key responsibilities will include:

·       Lead development efforts

·       Architecture planning and design

·       Support of the system

·       Work closely with VP of Product Development

·       Work with key team stakeholders to implement s technical  
strategy that maps to customer requirements.

The ideal candidate will have a track record of design, deployment and  
operation of a multi-channel multi-platform system.  Understand the  
concepts of a service-based architecture. You should have the ability  
to design and deliver on project and services with the understanding  
of the need to remain creative and proactive.

The position is based in New York and no telecommuting is available.

Skill set requirements

·       LAMP Stack

·       PHP

·       MySQL

·       Apache

·       Linux (ubuntu Server 7.04 / Red Hat)

·       SMS

·       Networking: TCP and UDP packets

·       SMTP

·       RSS

·       SMS/MMS

·       Strong expertise in Web Technologies

·       Strong OO experience

·       SaaS exposure

·       Knowledge of Web2.0 and Social Technologies

·       Knowledge of Mobile Technologies & Protocols

·       Expert knowledge in database design and implementation

·       Strong ability to multi task

·       Excellent communications skills (both written and verbal),  
interpersonal, and presentation skills.

·       Aptitude for creatively solving unstructured business problems

·       Minimum of 4 years of post-college experience directly  
relevant to the responsibilities described above.

·       Undergraduate degree in Computer Science from a top school is  
preferred.


Compensation

·       Annual salary consummate with experience and health care  
contribution.
