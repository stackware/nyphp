The NYPHP policy is that no commercial (including job) postings are to 
be made to the discussion lists.  The NYPHP Talk and SIG lists are for 
technical and community discussion.  The Jobs list was created for all 
job postings.

You can review the policies in the Charter available here:
    http://www.nyphp.org/content/about/charter.php
And the various lists and their purposes here:
    http://www.nyphp.org/mailinglists.php

Thanks!
