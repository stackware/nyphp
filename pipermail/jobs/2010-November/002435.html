New digital media start-up is looking for 2 Web Developers to
join our team in New York City. We are an exciting new startup that provides
an easy, fun-to-use place for folks to display and sell their digital 
creations, and see and purchase other folk's work as well. We have just

motivated, energetic and passionate specialists with tons of experience in 
marketing, strategy, entertainment and internet/web technology. We also provide
an extremely open, inclusive, collaborative, flexible and fun work environment
where every idea is a good idea and every team member is highly valued and
respected. Plus you get to shape the environment by joining our exciting team 
at an early stage.
* This is an on-site position at our Greenwich Village, Manhattan location.
* US Citizens or US Permanent Residents only - sorry, no Visa sponsorship.
* Compensation will be salary + equity
* No recruiters please.

How to Apply

----------------
>> As a Web Developer, you will work on adding exciting new features to

   iterative development cycle will require you to explore and learn new

   you the flexibility and opportunity to question the status quo and innovate 
   the website through your creativity and ideas.

   ensure future development will be built in a secure and scalable manner.
Experience
----------
>> Prior development experience building large-scale high-traffic and secure 
   consumer websites, in the social media / social networking / Web 2.0 space,

>> Extensive experience programming on the LAMP stack . Strong in PHP, MySQL, 
   Javascript frameworks such as JQuery, and AJAX
>> Experience integrating and working with Amazon Web Services (EC2, S3, RDS, 
   Cloudfront) and other cloud-based services
>> Experience creating custom integrations with multiple open source and 
   commercial social / web 2.0 technologies (Wordpress, Forums, Wikis, Facebook 
   connect etc.)
>> Optimising for the Mobile web (Android, iPhone, iPad etc) is a plus
>> Experience with Agile development and source control using SVN or GIT
>> Comfortable working in a team environment, including offshore team members
Skills
