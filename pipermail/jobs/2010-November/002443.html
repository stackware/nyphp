NYU Wagner is a path-breaking leadership school of public service in NYC 
and at New York University, a global network university.

Web Developer will be responsible for a) the overall design, 
maintenance, and functionality of the School's website and ad hoc 
programming projects, b) serving as a liaison with NYU's Information 
Technology Service's web server division, outside vendors and service

- Required: Bachelor's degree in computer science
- Preferred: Masters of Science degree in computer science or related 
discipline

- Minimum of 3 years experience programming complex websites, 
maintaining a website's access and security, and creating/modifying 
scripts or equivalent combination of education and experience.
- This position requires a solid understanding of cross-browser/platform 
issues and code solutions. The candidate must demonstrate a proven 
ability to monitor and analyze website problems, and also display 
excellent organizational, planning, verbal and written communication skills.

* Required - Advanced Knowledge/Skills in:
- PHP
- jQuery
- Javascript/AJAX
- MySQL
- HTML
- XHTML
- CSS
- Google Maps
- Linux
- Some experience with Java
- Adobe Photoshop

* Preferred Experience:
-The preferred candidate will have 5 plus years of experience in 
programming, website maintenance and development.
-Managerial experience is also preferred.

* Preferred Knowledge & Skills:
- Mobile website development experience
- Experience developing applications in Apex and Visualforce for the 
Salesforce.com platform
- Facebook APIs
- Adobe Illustrator
- Experience with Flash video players (JW Player)
