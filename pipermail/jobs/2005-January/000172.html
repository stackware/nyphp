Zend Technologies is looking for an instructor to teach an online course 
on “Using Zend Studio”.
The course will be three hours in length and taught once a month. This 
is a freelance position.

Responsibilities
- Create the syllabus and power point slides for a three hour course on 
Using Zend Studio.
- Make improvement to the course based on feedback.
- Teach the course once a month. The hours of the course will be 11:00am 
to 2:00pm EST.

Qualifications
- At least 6 months hands on experience using Zend Studio as your 
primary IDE.
- In depth understanding on all of Zend Studio’s features.
- Zend certified engineer, or plan on taking the certification test in 
the immediate future.
- Excellent communication skills.
- Excellent teaching and presentation skills.

Other
- You must have a broadband connection from the computer you will be 
using to teach the course.
- The classes are taught in an online virtual classroom environment 
which includes live audio.

How to Apply:

Please email a cover letter and your resume, with the subject line "Zend 
Studio Instructor" to ophir at zend.com .
In your cover letter, please tell us a little bit about yourself, 
detailing any relevant experience.
