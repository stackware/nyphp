Hi,
I have a position with a client in Northern NJ, a game development company.  Great company, great people.  The opening is full time, on site, no telecommute.  Location is not easily commutable from NYC or mass transit.  If you are interested, please forward your resume with contact info.

We need an experienced PHP Developer with full knowledge of LAMP (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP) web systems. This team member will work with the Product Development team to create the web and database parts of new social games on multiple platforms. The ideal candidate will have 2 - 4 years of web and database development experience, high technical aptitude, a positive team player attitude, and be excited and ready to be challenged technically and grow overall technical skills.

 

Qualifications:

 

BS in Computer Science (or similar concentration) or equivalent educational background.  

 

Candidates must have 2-4 years of PHP experience, including experience in the following:

 

  a.. PHP 4 and 5 with an emphasis on OOP and the use of design patterns. 
  b.. MySQL 
  c.. Javascript, CSS, HTML 
  d.. Strong understanding of web technologies and their implementation 
  e.. 3+ years experience building and supporting active web properties 
  f.. Ability to plan and execute several projects concurrently in a small-team environment 
  g.. Ability to work with technical and non-technical team members 
  h.. Debugging live applications
  i.. Writing applications to both create and parse XML documents
  j.. Writing statistics gathering applications
  k.. Writing modular, documented and efficient code
 

Absolutely Required Skills:  

 

PHP, HTML, CSS, XML, XHTML, Zend Studio or other design tool, MySQL, Linux/FreeBSD and Windows.

 

Helpful Skills:

 

Web game development, Flash, ActionScript, Facebook, Myspace, etc, application development.

 

Candidates MUST have a portfolio of previous sites developed and code samples available for review.  Applicants may be asked to perform a demonstration of their abilities or show developed code in order to assess technical knowledge.
