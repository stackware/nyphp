*WEB FRONT-END DEVELOPER  | New York University*
Washington Square Campus

The Digital Communications department of NYU is seeking a Web Front-end
Developer to among an internal team of professionals and occasional
third-party vendors to implement and maintain NYU.edu and NYU's related
online ventures. Responsibilities include the development of
standards-compliant, cross-browser code, fixing and resolving bugs/defects
in support of the functional design and success of highly visible,
enterprise, front-end Web properties.

*Qualifications/Required Education:* Bachelor's degree

*Required Experience:* 3-5+ years of hands-on experience developing dynamic
Web application interfaces, planning, developing, and modifying code to
implement provided designs, and proven experience working both
independently and collaboratively on a small team, or an equivalent
combination of education and experience.

*Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:* (include unique competencies,
certification, licenses, etc.): - Excellent planning, analytical, problem
solving, and communication skills; in particular the ability to communicate
technical details in a non-technical fashion
- Highly skilled with writing, clean, documented, and standards-compliant
(X)HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JSP, and other related programming languages
- Strong understanding of Web accessibility and search engine optimized
markup
- Familiar with Jquery-type code libraries
- Experience working with standard development tools used for code
versioning, bug tracking, documentation, etc.
- Ability to monitor and analyze Web site problems and suggest possible
solutions and priority levels
- Strong knowledge of a variety of current and emerging technologies
- Ability to prioritize, balance, and deliver quality work effectively
- Experience maintaining a professional work ethic and attitude, while
supporting and encouraging an enjoyable work environment

*Outstanding Benefits:*
As an employer, New York University is proud of the opportunities and
advantages it offers, and has a long-standing commitment to providing
high-quality employee benefits and programs that meet the needs of our
diverse community.

Benefits include: generous vacation package, work-life balance, choice of
several health coverage plans plus an NYU Dental Plan, access to the NYU
Retirement Plan, disability and life insurance, and more.

Full-time employees may also be eligible for tuition benefits for
themselves and their dependents.

To be considered, please apply to this position via NYUCareers.com
<http://www.nyucareers.com/applicants/Central?quickFind=58298>
