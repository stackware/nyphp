*Senior PHP Engineer*



Ready to step up and be the one and only PHP expert on a Python team?



A successful and rapidly growing startup has just acquired a company with a
tech product built entirely on PHP. They’re seeking a PHP expert to take
over the maintenance and build of this technology. You’ll be tasked with

built in Python.

market share. It's a great opportunity to get in early, but with a company
that is poised to succeed.



Competitive salary, benefits, stock options, flexible work schedule and a
great work environment. This is a permanent, full-time role.
