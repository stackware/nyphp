Senior OS Arch/Drupal Developer, NYC | 100-150k 

This is a full time, on-site, salaried position in New York City paying
$100,000 to $150,000 + benefits. US Citizens or green card holders only
please.  Local candidates preferred.  Thank you. 

Our client is a premier full-time employee based technology firm seeking
hands-on Sr. Open Source Architect/PM (PHP/Drupal) and Web Application
Development skills. Their growing Content Solutions Practice which caters to
a growing list of clients within the Media, Financial, Publishing,
Entertainment, Health industries in New York City. They are seeking talented
candidates to continue their steady growth.

The successful candidate must have great problem solving and communication
skills, act as a resource for junior developers, and be comfortable in a
hands-on capacity at all levels of the design, development, testing, and
deployment cycle in an agile mindset.

All candidates must possess a strong foundation in computer science and have
demonstrated a proven track record of sound code design, reuse, and
maintenance. Ideal candidates possess end-to-end knowledge of the content
management and web application space including performance and scalability
design and tuning, data migration, SEO strategy execution, CDN
implementation, metrics and third party web services integration, and
general knowledge of open source products and frameworks. In addition, the
successful candidate will have expertise in general system administration in
both Windows and Unix/Linux environments, database administration and
tuning, and web server configuration. The candidate will need to have the
following:

SKILLS & REQUIREMENTS: 
* Object-Oriented developer with PHP 5.x, Drupal 6.x/7 database and
distributed computing experience & theming 
* Project Full Life Cycle experience with Project Leader, Analyst,
Architect, Technical Lead skills is a plus 
* Working Knowledge of XHTML, CSS, Content Management applications 
* Understanding and knowledge of web development and content solutions 
* Experience within the Media or Publishing industry is a plus 
* Experience with CDN's and caching strategies is a plus 
* Experience administrating Linux, Apache or MySQL is a plus 
* Experience deploying web infrastructures is a plus 

Please submit your resume (preferably in MS-Word) and your salary
requirements to beau[AT]open-source-staffing.com
