Web Developer

Wouldn't it feel great to wake up each day knowing that you're part of a
dynamic organization that's on the cutting edge of the health revolution in
America? That's what the staff at The Institute for Integrative Nutrition
experiences, and you could too!

Some of the benefits you'll get while working at Integrative Nutrition
include:
.	Beautiful, centrally located modern offices with a natural work
environment
.	Working with like-minded, health-oriented colleagues
.	Organic breakfast, lunch, and afternoon snacks
.	An office that bonds together over lunch every day!
.	Opportunity to join staff retreats to destinations like Kripalu
.	Quarterly donations to a staff-selected charity on IIN's behalf 
.	Full health care benefits: health, dental, disability, vision, life
.	Monthly unlimited metro pass (or similar transit equivalent)
.	401K with 4% employer contribution
.	Paid vacation days
.	Paid major holidays
.	Your own health coach
.	Complimentary in-house yoga classes
.	Biweekly chair massages
.	Monthly chiropractic adjustments
.	Fresh flowers on your desk
.	Staff concierge service

Responsibilities:
.	Expand/Improve IIN's web presence
.	Develop new websites to spec and maintain them going forward
.	Provide input to help improve site architecture, layout, and content
.	Assist in optimizing website for increased opt-ins, better search
rankings, and user engagement and retention
.	Follow best coding practices of our CMS (Drupal)
.	Test for cross-browser, cross-platform, and/or cross-device
compatibility
.	Work through Salesforce issues with internal SF team and developer
.	Form coding, form delivery, creation of new fields on landing pages
.	Identifying lead sources, initial contacts and creating new
campaigns for landing pages and form on the site 

Implement website updates:
.	Implement website improvements
.	Perform ongoing QA for website
.	Build landing pages, microsites, and webpages
.	Build pages for site with text, audio/video, photos, with
consideration of appropriate compression techniques, resolution, sizes,
color maps, and depths to ensure delivery of high quality website product
.	Work with creative team to update language, photos and branded
content

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:
.	Junior PHP developer
.	Drupal or other CMS experience
.	Front-end skills including Javascript, HTML and CSS (including
HTML5)
.	jQuery experience helpful
.	Basic design sense and image editing ability
.	Familiarity with SVN for code management
.	Basic understanding of Apache webserver and its administration
.	Experience implementing web pages from wireframes and/or design
comps
.	Have teamwork skills and are self-starting
.	Comfortable with semi-agile development, with competing projects and
deadlines
.	Are flexible, fast and reliable in your work

Competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package, with full insurance
coverage and 401(k). 

Location is in Flatiron District of NYC.
 
If this sounds like the kind of opportunity and challenge you're up for,
then we invite you to fill out a job application at the link below:

http://www.integrativenutrition.com/about/jobs/

We hope to welcome you to your new job soon!

Please, no recruiters or solicitations.
