[JOB #1] PHP/Symfony Developer - NYC | 70-100k  

This is a full time, on-site, salaried PHP/Symfony Developer position
located in New York City paying a salary of $70,000 to $100,000 depending on
experience + benefits. No telecommuting allowed. US Citizens or Green Card
holders only please. Local candidates only. Full company details provided to
qualified applicants. Thank you. 

Our New York City start-up client the first global movie theater. Currently
in beta, they show feature length films for live social audiences online.
With deep relationships in Hollywood and access to top content including
both independent and studio films, they are well-positioned to become a
disruptive force in the media distribution and exhibition businesses, and is
already generating significant revenues pre-launch. Seed funding in place.  

We are looking for a developer who is honest, team-oriented, hard-working,
and highly motivated. New hires will be members of a small, intensely
focused team iterating quickly on a complex product. We want someone who is
highly skilled, excited by a challenge and the prospect of making something
new.  The ideal candidate would have expertise across a range of languages
and frameworks:  

REQUIRED: PHP, Symfony, CSS, JavaScript, HTML 

DESIRED: Python Tornado, Amazon Web Services; MongoDB, Solr. Knowledge of
video streaming technologies is also highly desirable.

If you are interested in this job, please submit your RESUME and SALARY
requirement to opensourcestaffing|AT|gmail.com

* Experience with an MVC framework (PREFERABLY Symfony)

* Thorough understanding of Social Networking and Web 2.0 technologies

* Ability to work in a small team and build consensus with colleagues
