*Senior Engineer - competitive salary*



The Senior Engineer will focus on new feature development and maintenance
tasks and for various aspects of our internal products (such as our CRM and
reporting tools) and external products (such as our client-facing websites).



Requirements:

   - Well-versed in all aspects of web development from back end (data
   design, security, scalability) to front-end (usability and cross-browser
   compatibility) and be able to leverage these skills to architect solutions
   for projects.


   - Ability to interact with project team to clarify requirements as
   needed to turn specifications into working product on schedule.


   - Have 5 or more years of experience doing medium to large scale web
   development projects and maintenance.

Programming Languages we work with:


   - PHP
   - Javascript
   - SQL (MySQL, SQLite, SQL Server)
   - Python
   - Linux shell (bash etc.)

 Environments/Frameworks we work with:

   - LAMP (Linux, Apache, PHP, MySQL)
   - WordPress
   - Apache Tomcat ( Solr, Lucene )
   - Windows Server ( Red Dot, MS Dynamics Great Plains)
   - Subversion
   - Django
   - jQuery
   - SugarCRM


Qualified candidates should send a cover letter, resume, and code samples
to RGEjobs at roubini.com with “Senior Engineer” as the subject of the email.


* Recruiters: please do not contact us regarding this opportunity.



*About Roubini Global Economics*



Roubini Global Economics (RGE) is an independent, global macroeconomic
strategy research firm founded in 2004 by renowned economist Nouriel
Roubini. RGE research interprets global economic signals into practical
macro-strategy insight for a wide range of financial and policy

economies and markets by illustrating vulnerabilities and risks; giving
