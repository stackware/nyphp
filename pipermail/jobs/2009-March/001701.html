GENERAL RESPONSIBILITIES      
We are currently seeking someone for a 9 month consulting position to work in our CT office. ·         Performs other duties as assigned.

·         5-10 years of experience in the field with a Bachelor's Degree or equivalent combinations of advanced degrees and experience required

·         C++ and Java working experience in Linux/UNIX environment required

·         Advanced user experience in Linux and Solaris required

·         Experience with configuring web servers (Apache or Tomcat) highly recommended
