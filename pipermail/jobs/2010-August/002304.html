E-commerce PHP Developer

Hey Internet rockstar. JackThreads.com, a members-only
online retailer offering discounted merchandise from
top-tier men's streetwear, contemporary fashion, and
lifestyle brands, has an immediate opening for an
experienced E-commerce PHP Developer to join our small and
growing development team, under the Director of Technology.

Requirements:

- Bachelors degree in CS or related field, or equivalent
experience.
- 3+ years PHP experience, CakePHP a big plus
- 1+ years jQuery
- Database proficiency required
- E-commerce experience preferred
- Familiarity with a UNIX environment required, sys admin
skills a big plus
- Familiarity with CSS required, zen master skills a huge plus.
- Bonus points for familiarity with the pitfalls of email
campaigns and reputation management

The position requires both design and implementation work.
We've got a bunch of cool applications in development, some
large, some small, some frontend, some backend. You'll work
hard, and you'll learn a lot.

Candidates must be able to work full-time from our offices
in either Columbus, OH or Soho, NYC.

Compensation commensurate with experience.

Join up: send resume and cover blurb to techjobs at jackthreads.com
with E-commerce PHP Developer in the subject line.
