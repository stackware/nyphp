We are looking for a *talented web developer* to help us build our consumer
website using Drupal as a CMS platform.



*JOB RESPONSIBILITIES*

You will be responsible for building our public-facing corporate website
using Drupal as a CMS and writing custom HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP when
required to support our already designed interface.  Primary
responsibilities will include translating Photoshop comps into functional
pages, configuring and tweaking Drupal to support our business requirements,
integrating AJAX elements and Flash Video into the site, and providing
continued support for the website once launched.



This is an excellent opportunity for someone with prior experience
developing amazing consumer-oriented websites that blend strong functional
web usability, core SEO principles and impeccable markup.  You will work
hand-in-hand with a talented group of professionals on a full-time basis in
our SoHo office during the initial development of the site.



---------------------------



*EXPERIENCE AND NECESSARY SKILLS*



·         In-depth knowledge of Drupal, with at least 3 years experience
creating custom Drupal websites.

·         5+ years of general experience developing consumer-facing
websites.

·         Expert proficiency with XHTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

·         Experience developing web applications with dynamic pages.

·         Understanding of web usability best practices, SEO, interface
design, and standards compliant markup.

·         Proven ability to translate designer-produced Photoshop comps into
fully rendered, grid-based sites.

·         Experience with flash video integration.

·         Knowledge of JavaScript libraries such as jQuery or YUI.

·         Strong PHP skills.

·         Excellent verbal and visual communication skills.

·         Creativity, conscientiousness, and meticulous attention to detail.

·         Familiarity with Photoshop, Actionscript, and MySQL a plus.



*To apply, please email all of the following:*

1)      A brief letter explaining why you believe you are an exceptionally
good fit for this position. Please use “Drupal Web Developer” as the subject
of your application email.

2)      A link to an online portfolio that we can browse through, or links
to multiple sites you have developed.

3)      Your resume in the body of the email.



*Applications without a cover letter and portfolio will not be considered.*
