·         Experience with Drupal (or strong desire to learn)

·         Extensive knowledge of LAMP (Linux-Apache-MySQL-PHP) based development

·         Experience working with Web Services (e.g. REST, SOAP) in a highly scalable, highly available environment

Skills:

·         Strong fundamental understanding of web application technology

·         Strong understanding of basic web skills (HTML, CSS and Javascript)

·         Good communications skills and organization skills

·         Ability and desire to learn new applications/processes/environments
