Hi everyone, 

Krossover is looking for a PHP developer.

First a bit about us:
Krossover (www.krossover.com) is a sports media company. We break down game film for coaches and athletes, saving them 4-6 hours or work per week.  We make our customers very happy, and all of our first year customers are coming back for more.  We are a funded startup, and expect rapid growth in the near future.  Our ultimate goal is to be the YouTube of sports, but in the meantime we provide a real service to people who need it.

From a technology point of view we're doing lots of cool projects involving video, content sharing, and more. 

In addition to a PHP developer we're also looking for a front-end developer.

To apply send me (alex at krossover.com) your resume.

Job spec are:

PHP Web Developer
-----------------
Job Description

- Responsible for developing complex, scalable and robust web portal.

- Experience in developing robust PHP code using framework (like Yii / Kohana) is highly desirable

Qualifications: 
- At least BS in Computer Science or related field
- 2 years of experience

Knowledge and Skill Requirements
- Worked on multi-tiered architecture

- Solid understanding of MVC
- Database schema design skills and knowledge of nosql DB like MongoDB
- Hands on experience in JQuery, Javascript, AJAX, XML
- Knowledge of security, web services, cross browser issues
- Experience of handling heavily trafficked web portals
- Good in problem solving and analytical thinking
- Good communication skills
- Self directed, highly motivated individual willing to face technical challenges
- Experience in html5 and/or video streaming technology is a plus




Lead Front End Developer
-------------------------
- Javascript Ninja
- Solid experience with HTML, CSS, Media Object Embedding
- Hands-on bleeding edge technologies including HTML5, CSS3
- Comfortable working with Json, Ajax
- Some example of smart utilization of existing Javascript based libraries (e.g. Jquery UI)
- Ability to work with Photoshop desirable


Please let me know if you have any questions.
