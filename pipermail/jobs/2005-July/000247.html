Watershed Web Design, a full service web development company, seeks a
freelance PHP/MySQL programmer to lead an e-commerce project.

The ideal candidate must have a minimum of 3 years' experience in PHP/MySQL,
DHTML, Javascript, XML and CSS.  

Candidates must have:

* Strong coding skills - we are looking for a programmer not a designer
* Experience leading development teams
* The ability to create technical specifications
* Experience designing databases
* The ability to separate content from functionality and business logic
* The ability to turn graphic designs into functional, CSS compliant web
sites. 
* Experience with MySQL/PHP.
* Familiarity with source code control, such as cvs
* Excellent verbal and written skills in English
* Experience with Apache and its support of PHP

Some experience with osCommerce or Zen Cart is desirable but not required.

We are based in the Princeton area.  The project will be developed offsite,
but interested candidates should be based in the NYC, NJ, Phila. region.  

We have several projects in the pipeline and we are looking for someone with
whom we can work with on these future projects as well. This position has
the potential to turn into a full time arrangement.

Compensation is commensurate with experience.

E-mail your resumes, works samples and rate to
mailto:jobs at watersheddesign.com. Include a cover letter outlining your
skills and experience in the email. Applicants only.  No recruiters please.
