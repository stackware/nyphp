Job Type:                   Flash Development and Graphic Design

Term:                         Immediate Hire

Description:               

 

Small sized game development firm is seeking mid-level Flash
Designer/Developer for exciting game development projects. The designer will

meet company and client expectations and overall project goals. You will

technical skills.

 

We offer great benefits and an energetic, creative, and fun environment to
be a part of.

 

Qualifications:

 

BA in Graphic Design (or similar concentration) or equivalent educational
background.  

 

A diverse and polished portfolio is required before interview.  Candidates
must have 2 - 5 years graphic design experience, and 1 - 3 years multimedia
design experience. Must have a demonstrated understanding of multimedia and
web integration issues, as well as knowledge of usability principles.
Necessary skills also include Macromedia Flash, Flash MX Professional 2004,
Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Illustrator, ImageReady, PhotoShop, ActionScript
2.0, HTML, PHP, and XML.  Experience with animation in Flash a major PLUS!

 

Required Skills:         

 

Graphics, Multimedia creativity, Flash, Dreamweaver, Fireworks, Illustrator,
