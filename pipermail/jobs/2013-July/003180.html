This is a full time, on-site, salaried LAMP Developer position located in

Join an exciting online advertising technology company based in Brooklyn
NY. Backed by the same investors behind Instagram and Twitter, we are
generating revenue, profitable, and poised for continued success. Great

engineers who love solving challenging, real-world problems. Top of the

* Solid experience building scalable web applications from the ground up on
a LAMP stack
* Fluent in PHP or Python (or both!)
* Expertise writing efficient, complex SQL statements by hand
* Experience with jQuery or similar Javascript libraries
* Ability to work independently and think outside the box to solve problems
* Strong math background / experience working with data strongly preferred
* Familiarity with MongoDB, CodeIgniter and/or Django is helpful, but not
required

If you are interested in this job, please submit your resume and salary
