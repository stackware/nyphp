Senior OO PHP GUI Developer - NYC/Flatiron District
Employee Opportunity (not a contract)

You will work on projects on our front end, collaborating with other developers throughout the requirements, design, and implementation phases of projects. Ideally you will have strong object-oriented PHP skills; JavaScript, XML/XSLT, CSS, and database (SQL) experience; and outstanding written and oral communications skills, including the ability to communicate well with non-technical associates. Backend is heavy OO Perl.

Specifically, you will offer:

*Strong understanding of object oriented systems and UI design principles are required
*3-5+ years experience with object-oriented PHP writing robust software systems, using JavaScript/AJAX and XML/XSLT
*3+ years experience with SQL, CSS
*Experience with PostgreSQL, Smarty templates garner Bonus Points
*Familiarity with major databases (Oracle, MS SQL Server) are major Bonus Points
*Got Perl? Excellent!
*Team lead experience? Very cool!

The work environment: Located in the Flatiron District of New York, easily accessible from many directions, our mission is to transform the way organizations manage their database infrastructure through the use of automation. This is not a 24/7 position; however, there may be times when you have to put in the hours but you certainly won’t be calling in for pizza delivery at 4:00 AM. Our entire team is top shelf with a myriad of personal interests that always seem to find their way into our daily activities. Very programmer casual.

While you’re here, browse through our site <http://jobs.gridapp.com> and learn more about our products and services and read for yourself how we help companies manage their data resources smarter. And be sure to read our employee-written GridApp blog <http://blog.gridapp.com/> .
