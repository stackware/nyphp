Interwoven, a world-renowned leader in Enterprise Software Solutions, is
currently looking to fill a Director of Engineering position on our
Optimost product line. The individual will be responsible for designing,
building and maintaining Optimost technologies. More specifically he/she
will: 

 

* Mentor and manage our talented team of software developers in the
architecture and implementation of sound and solid, scalable code.
* Work with the other technical teams throughout the organization to
develop best practices, procedures and guidelines. 
* Guide the technical direction of the company through expertise and
experience.
* Design and develop software in a highly efficient, simple, scalable
environment

* Work with Business leaders across the organization to help prioritize
our product development roadmap

* Recruit other top talent

What's in it for you:

* The opportunity to work with an innovative product

* Tons of growth potential in a stable, fast-paced start-up environment
* Great benefits starting on your first day of employment
* Competitive salary

 

Required Skills:  

 

* 10+ years of Software Development experience
* 5+ years hands-on engineering management or software architecture
experience
* HTML, JavaScript, Linux, MySQL and PHP 
* SOA or REST experience desired 
* JSON with XML or AJAX, preferably with serialization experience 
* Basic knowledge of server tuning or optimization required
* AGILE and SCRUM methodologies preferred
* Bachelors or Masters degree in Computer Science or similar field. 

 

So, if you're a hands-on manager or architect with web-based software
experience, apply now!
