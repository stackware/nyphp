Senior PHP/LAMP Developer

Are you a Sr. PHP/LAMP developer with agency experience and a portfolio 
that includes commercial websites and scalable web applications? Are you 
looking for a big break where you work on challenging and interesting 
problems in the company of like-minded people? We'd like to meet you.

What We're Looking For
-    4+ years of PHP/LAMP development
-    2+ years of Linux system administration
-    2+ years of MySQL database administration
-    Experience with providing web services to Flash Applications (AMF & 
Remoting)
-    Experience with JavaScript, XML, CSS, HTML/XHTML
-    Knowledge of Object Oriented Design and MVC frameworks
-    Ability to thoroughly document your work
-    Clean, smart code

You Get Bonus Points For
-    Developing for E-commerce
-    Experience with Flex and ActionScript
-    Experience with version control systems
-    Experience with RubyOnRails and/or ASP or .NET
-    Active participation in the Open Source community

Come Play With Us
Domani Studios is a relaxed bunch of talented developers, interaction 
designers and strategists. We're okay with calling ourselves talented 
because we're cool with people who have pride in their work. Plus we 
have the results and fancy awards to back up our statement. Check it out 
here: http://www.domanistudios.com.

We've Got Game
The fridge is stocked. The coffee is tasty. And you're invited for a 
friendly game of darts, Guitar Hero, FIFA or Wii tennis. You may as well 
be comfortable and have fun while you're here, right? That's why we 
offer health, dental and vision for you and your family, 401K, plus 
pre-tax commuter benefits, and a super chill atmosphere.

We'll Respect Your Talent
We're like you; we want to learn and grow. Ambition is nurtured and 
rewarded here. All of our developers get their hands dirty creating 
various rich media interfaces including robust web applications, banners 
and websites. Those with senior skills are involved in planning, 
managing and mentoring.

Interested?
Candidate must be eligible to work in the Unites States. Please send 
your résumé and URLs, or online portfolio, to 
workwithus at domanistudios.com with SR. PHP/LAMP DEVELOPER in the subject 
line of your email. This is a full-time position located in DUMBO, 
Brooklyn, New York.
