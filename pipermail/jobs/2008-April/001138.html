We are looking for an additional MVC/PHP programmer to work in conjunction
with another freelance (not side by side, but on different projects
simultaneously). Our website is www.tungstenproperty.com – we need someone
to help us:

 1. Add/change various features to the Public Site - Specifically speeding
up the General Uploading and Rendering of the site, its annoying slow at
timeso compared to our competiors and we seem to have trouble finding
someone that can help diagnose the problem.  Additionally, we would like to
structure a new sub template that allows sub domain pages to be easily
integrated into the main site with basic HTML coding.  Intergration of
Google Maps into the site.  Long term we need to work on a more robust
search/sort system.  We are looking for something very simple and fast to
navigate, any proposals would be welcome.
 2. Maintain and improve our current multi-user (user friendly) database,
which is based on a series of the language based below (I am
 not sure which one myself) - Including expanding number of out source

Requirements:
* You should have considerable experience with PHP and with MYSQL, LAMP
stack
* You should be able to work with and adjust to other peoples code. We have
heard that our code is fairly "clean", however I am not a programmer and
imagine this more a matter of opinion than fact.  Nevertheless, you will

* You must be VERY reachable; we have had the unfortunate experience of not

deadlines for yourself, which we will expect you to uphold, and that you
have the discipline to keep them. If you like, you can provide us with
references that highlight your ability to do this.  We are interested in a

a project for three days and $100, we will pay you $120 if complete in two
days, $90 if you complete in 4 days, $80 if you complete in 3 days, etc.  We
are open to any creative structure you may suggest as well.
* A professional/diligent attitude to work without losing your creative
ability.
* Languages (if you don't know every single one it can probably still be
figured out):
