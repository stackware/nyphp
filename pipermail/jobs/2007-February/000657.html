Seeking 2-3 strong LAMP Developers who can "hit the ground running". This will be a minimum of a 3 month assignment with a very good possibility of extension of up to 6-9 months. Good working environment in a .com type atmosphere within a small group. This is a high volume type website and you should have that type of experience. 

 

You must have extensive experience programming in a UNIX/Linux environment. You are a self starter who will pick up programming tasks and run with them, collaborating in a team environment with buy-in, checkpoints and testing.

 

Necessary skills include the following: 
B.S./B.A. in Computer Science, or a degree in a related field plus 
Computer Science coursework, or equivalent experience 
Over 6 years Web development work experience 
At least 4 years PHP 4-5 experience in an Apache, Linux, MySQL 
environment 
Strong knowledge of MySQL, performance issues, and feature trade-offs 
Experience with high traffic websites 
In-depth knowledge of HTML, CSS and the major web browser platforms 
Object-oriented design in PHP 
Experience with XML, XML-RPC, XSLT and JSON 
Text parsing, regular expressions, validation rules, and security. 
Experience working as part of a team 
Strong verbal and written communication skills 
Formal software development methodology 
Experience writing shared and reusable components of code 
Experience designing and tuning applications that can scale 
Some background in another structured programming language, such as Java 
or C/C++ 
Use of source control (CVS, SVN) 
UNIX/Linux: Proficiency as a system user and light administration 
skills.

 

 

Day-Day:

 

We are seeking a candidate who is confident about building and extending 
the backend of a personalization platform for  a site with an 
unparalleled combination of scale and complexity.  

Responsibilities include the following: 
Developing key PHP classes and components for the server side of an AJAX 
platform Considering client, protocol, server, performance and security issues 
during development Contributing creative ideas toward solving platform issues as they crop 
up Designing reusable, object-oriented code that can be redeployed Ensuring robustness and performance goals through unit and performance testing.

 

Right now we are rearchitecting all existing areas of our site using LAMP. Originally the site was built using a combination of SUN/LINUX, Oracle, Perl, a proprietary scripting language and Fatwire which is our current content management system. In addition to the rearchitecture, they are adding on new sections to the site which include Health, Style, T-Online, Business & Technology, Analytics, and we're also doing new work within our Search group. Most likely, you will be involved in new development. 

Count on working on rearchitecture and new development of a high traffic/high performance website.
