Naviance (www.naviance.com) has a full-time opening for an  
experienced, advanced level LAMP web developer in our Washington, DC  
office. We are looking for an individual with a proven track record  
delivering well-designed PHP code for web-based applications in  
complex domains (especially workflow or CRM type systems). Developers  
will be responsible for maintaining and enhancing our core product  
platform as well as developing new modules from the ground up. We  
have a very complex, multi-faceted PHP application that provides a  
lot of opportunity to use many different skills as a developer.

Desired background and skills include:
- Professional development on a real-world PHP application of  
moderate complexity and/or high traffic
- PHP 5 and OO experience
- MySQL 4+
- Familiarity with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, XML
- Familiarity with basic Linux/Apache administration
- PHP command line scripting
- Subversion
- Test driven development
- Zend Platform and/or PHP configuration management
- Knowledge of database design and query optimization techniques

A plus if you have expertise in: web services, high volume PDF  
processing, high volume email processing, network administration,  
database administration, enterprise data munging, AJAX, JAVA, student  
information systems

We are a fast-growing and profitable company with offices in the  
heart of downtown DC close to multiple metro lines. Our team is small  
and dress is very casual.

To apply: Please submit your cover letter and resume to  
careers at naviance.com. Please indicate the title of the position for  
which you are applying. Resumes without cover letters will not be  
considered. We do not contract or sponsor.
