REQUIRED Skills

- Required Competencies: LAMP, PHP, MySQL, XML, XHTML, JavaScript, CSS, CVS,
Linux

- Demonstrated expertise in developing and deploying client/server

lifecycle

- Strong communication and technical writing skills
- Exceptional problem solving skills a must

BENEFICIAL Skills

- Microsoft-centric technologies and languages (such as ASP and .Net)

- Enterprise-level RDBMS software
- Quality Assurance testing
