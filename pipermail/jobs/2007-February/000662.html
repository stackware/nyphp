My client is looking for a Senior LAMP Developer for their New York
office.

 

Qualifications

 

This is a hands-on development position requiring the following:

 

*	Minimum 3 years experience using object-oriented Perl building
user-facing applications 
*	Proficiency in JavaScript, with heavy experience in
object-oriented JavaScript development 
*	Experience in developing mod_perl compliant applications, with
extensive experience porting CGI applications to mod_perl preferred. 
*	Working knowledge with CGI::Application, Catalyst, or other MVC
Perl development framework 
*	Working knowledge of AJAX 
*	Experience with Python or PHP a strong plus 
*	At least 3-5 years experience in full software development
lifecycle 
*	Solid understanding of Apache 1.3 and 2.0 operations and
integration in a Linux/Unix environment 
*	Familiarity with basic Linux systems administration 
*	Development experience with the MySQL platform 
*	Experience working with open source tools and applications, with
prior participation and contribution in open source projects a plus. 
*	Expert knowledge of XML, XSLT, SOAP, CSS, DHTML, and XHTML 
*	Experience in interactive media preferred
