Open Source Developers 

A large online publisher has immediate opening for a talented and motivated
technical lead to join a fun, energetic, ethnic and established Internet
Company located in downtown Manhattan.  This successful online publisher has
over 16 million members and is a profitable start-up. The technology
framework is based on PHP, MySQL, and Oracle.  Comprised of over 1 million
lines of PHP code and receiving over twenty million dynamic page requests
per day, their sites are among the largest and most heavily trafficked PHP
sites in the world and our technologists are the leading experts in
developing high performing, large scale enterprise applications.

 

Job Description:
We're looking for developers for our client who are skilled in an enterprise
environment and understand not only how to build good technology, but also
how to build scalable technology.  They want to expand their team with
self-motivated creative people who thrive in an environment where they are
constantly challenged.  The developer will focus on core API's and
application architecture.  


Skills / Qualifications:

- Three to five years of experience in software and web application
development

- Advanced understanding of MySQL and Oracle databases (developer
fundamentals)

- Thorough understanding of programming concepts and principles

- General understanding of the C language

- Good understanding of the PHP programming language, XHTML/CSS, and
ECMAScript.

- Advanced knowledge of web protocols and programming requirements

- Advanced knowledge of a UNIX-type operating system (preferably Linux)

- Willingness to lead in a structured team environment

- Experience building highly interactive, highly trafficked, database-driven
web applications

 

Responsibilities:

- Development and enhancement of core site APIs

- Assisting lead developers in creating and implementing project
architecture and requirements

- Adhering to and contributing to established CCI coding guidelines and
standards

- Ensuring quality of software produced through peer code review

- Creating work estimates and timeframes for development tasks

- Providing guidance to Associate Developers
