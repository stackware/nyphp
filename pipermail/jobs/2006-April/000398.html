Web Developer, Village Voice Digital

The Village Voice, one of Village Voice Media's publications, seeks an experienced developer to work with us on a full-time basis to build and enhance small and large-scale applications for our publications' websites and internal products. We seek a motivated and creative individual who can take ownership of a few key projects and see them through to completion.

Strong and demonstrable skills in PHP, Perl, MySQL, XML, RSS, Javascript and CSS are required. Candidates must be comfortable at the *nix command line. Experience with bash shell programming, Apache, Db administration, mod_rewrite, Subversion/CVS, Python and ActionScript is highly desirable.

The ideal candidate will have experience working in publishing and new media environments and will have familiarity with the technical requirements and pitfalls of high traffic web sites. The ideal candidate will also possess excellent customer service skills, excellent written and verbal communication skills and strong organizational and time management skills.

Village Voice Media offers a comprehensive benefits package, with opportunity for career growth.

Please email your resume and cover letter, including salary requirements to Wellington Fan, Director of Development at wfan <AT> villagevoice <DOT> com. Please be sure to use the subject line: "Web Developer, Village Voice Digital".
