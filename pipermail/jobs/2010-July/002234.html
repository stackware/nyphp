Etsy is hiring.  We have a video about it.

http://codeascraft.etsy.com/2010/07/09/etsy-is-hiring/

We continue to search for the greatest engineers in New York City.  Our
platform makes use of many different technologies including PHP, Python,
Ruby, Scala, Javascript, Clojure, C, Java, and Matlab. Yes, Matlab. So as
you can see we need people who, ideally, already know everything. Or failing
that, are capable of learning anything.

Comparing Etsy to other places:
* Automated testing is more important to us.
* Job titles and "N years of experience" are less important to us.
* Engineers are expected to be able to manage themselves to a greater
extent.
* You will need to learn more about operations (see:
http://codeascraft.etsycorp.com/2010/02/17/dev-ops-cooperation/).

Dogs are part of the team, and are expected to commit code.

Read about open positions here:
http://hire.jobvite.com/Jobvite/Jobs.aspx?m=n1BMFfwN
