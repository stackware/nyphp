Web Developer

MiMedia, LLC is a venture-backed, rapidly growing tech startup and is seeking a highly experienced full time Web Developer who is interested in building the first personal cloud storage platform.

This position will report directly to the VP of Development and will be responsible for the development of our product Web-interfaces. Ideal candidates will have in-depth experience using JSON, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AJAX and some experience with Flash, Actionscript 3.0.

Responsibilities
- Develop our Web-based Personal Media Portal
- Work with designers to implement the UI Look and Feel
- Integrate the Portal with Leading Social Networks and Web Sites

Qualifications and Experience
- 5+ Years of Web Development Experience
- Expert using XML, and JSON to interface with web services
- Expert with HTML and CSS, with knowledge of HTML5
- Expert with JavaScript and AJAX (jQuery etc.)
- Experience with Adobe Flash and ActionScript 3.0
- Experience with PHP5 and object oriented code design
- Experience Developing High-Performance, Scalable Systems
- Comfortable in a Startup, Agile and Extreme Programming Environment

Compensation
- $90k or Commensurate With Experience
- Full Benefits with Medical, Dental and 401k
- Equity Participation

About MiMedia 
MiMedia's online personal media platform enables consumers to secure and enjoy their digital life. We are in the process of assembling the final members of our Port Chester NY based core development team. MiMedia is searching for exceptionally talented individuals who are interested in a unique opportunity to join us at an early stage and have a large impact on our business.

Qualified candidates should submit resumes to shari at mimedia.com.
