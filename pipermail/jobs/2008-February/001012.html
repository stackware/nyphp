Freelance Web Developer Needed – NY
 
Silver Communications, a successful branding and design New York agency with a financial industry client base, is looking for a short-term to long-term freelance Web Developer. A successful candidate for this position is an exceptionally well-organized self-starter who has a strong sense of responsibility and works well as part of a team. You would be involved in a number of various projects helping our clients strengthen their web presence.

Skills
-	Minimum 4-5 years experience in web development, preferably for institutional clients, professional community sites, and e-commerce products.
-	Significant track record in transforming design mock-ups into highly usable HTML/CSS/JavaScript/DHTML/PHP/ASP websites.
-	Expertise in producing W3 compliant, cross-platform and cross-browser compatible code.
-	Experience developing interfaces intended for high-traffic online destinations with users of a variety of skills and levels. 
-	User Interface/User Interaction expertise. 
-	Ability to integrate HTML/CSS front ends with backend systems.
-	Working knowledge of common CMS
-	Understanding of design basics and principles.
-	Meticulous attention to detail.

Software
-	Flash/ActionScript (Expert level) 
-	Strong experience with XHTML, CSS, HTML, XML, AJAX, DHTML, JavaScript, Flex, ASP
-	Ability to develop entire site in PHP
-	SQL/mySQL


Please send your resume and links to work samples to info at silvercomm.com. Candidates who are not US citizens or permanent residents need to have authorization to work in the US in order to apply. NY area residents only.
