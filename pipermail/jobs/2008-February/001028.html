Position: Web Developer

 

Please note: This is an in-office position in New York City. No inquiries
from agencies or outsourcing specialists, please. 

 

Position Summary 
EuroCheapo.com, an online travel guide, seeks a Web Developer to join other
travel nuts in our office in New York City. The primary scope of this
position is to assist in developing new community features including site
membership, user reviews, forums, and other user generated content
applications. This position will work in tandem with our senior developers
and also help enhance existing website functionality, assist with SEO
efforts, and work closely with our editorial and marketing departments.  

 

Applicant Requirements 

- Experience building object-oriented web sites in PHP  

- Understanding of relational databases and SQL

- An appreciation of independent travel

Desirable but not Essential 

- Hands-on knowledge of cross-platform web development (HTML, CSS,
Javascript, AJAX, XML)

- Proficiency with Photoshop

- Experience creating third-party applications for Facebook and / or
OpenSocial

- Experience in creating membership-based community applications, including
user reviews, forums, ratings and similar features

 

Position Details 

This is an onsite position, starting at 25-30 hours per week, with the
potential to become a full-time employee with full benefits. EuroCheapo's
office is located in SoHo.

 

Interested applicants should submit a resume (including URLs of websites
developed) along with a cover letter stating hourly rate.  

 

Our Company 

EuroCheapo.com is an Internet guide to budget travel in Europe. Led by a
team of travel experts in New York City and correspondents throughout
Europe, EuroCheapo.com helps travelers find the best budget accommodations
in Europe and provides detailed city guide information for European cities.
EuroCheapo's meta-search engine, "CheapoSearch" combines in-house editorial
reviews with hotel rate information from multiple suppliers for powerful
price comparison. EuroCheapo has been featured in the New York Times, USA
Today, Chicago Tribune, Washington Post, Budget Travel magazine, and was
recently named one of PC Magazine's "Top 100 Undiscovered Web Sites".
