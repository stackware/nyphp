NPower (www.npower.org<http://www.npower.org>) is a nonprofit technology organization serving other nonprofit organizations. We are in need of a skilled PHP programmer on a paid, subcontractor basis to perform a code audit and potentially partner for future development/

The audit is of a custom built community site for a national nonprofit who places college students in local highschools to teach health education courses.
