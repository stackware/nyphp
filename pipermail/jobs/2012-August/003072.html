Web Developer (PHP) - New York, NY - Up to 90,000

The PHP Developer works on a project-by-project basis with other engineers,
web developers, business and marketing team members to create an intuitive
experience for website visitors and clients. This includes extending and
improving an object-oriented PHP framework, building solutions and
maintaining existing applications in a high-energy environment.

Primary Duties and Responsibilities:

*	Build Great Web Sites: work with other engineers, developers,
architects, QA, system engineers and project managers in solving new and
existing technical challenges.
*	Talk the Talk, Walk the Walk: understand requirements, implement
solutions, and provide administration tools and documentation for specific
projects.
*	Be There When Needed: perform scheduled on-call duties.
*	Speak Up: participate in Design and Code Reviews.
*	Find A Better Way: follow and enhance existing development
processes; Measure and verify that new and old code has acceptable
performance and scalability; Maintain ownership of the projects assigned.
*	Get It Done: deliver accurate estimates, provide regular feedback
and consistently meet project deadlines.

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:

*	Exceptional Object Oriented PHP Development Experience: four (4) +
years of professional-level OO PHP 5 experience, including experience with
MVC Frameworks.
*	Plays Nice With Others: collaborative development experience, able
to explain complex ideas and concepts to non-technical team members.
*	Unit Testing Makes You Happy: understand how and why unit testing
delivers better solutions and speeds up maintenance.
*	Laser Vision: superb attention to detail.
*	Experience with Web Standards philosophies and modern XHTML/HTML
/CSS markup recommended.
*	Related experience in software testing and design desired.
*	Team-oriented experience highly desired.
*	Excellent oral and written communication skills regardless of
audience.

Minimum Education/Experience Requirements:
Bachelor's degree in computer science, software development, art and
technology, information technology, information systems or equivalent work
experience.
Established portfolio of work and/or code samples.
