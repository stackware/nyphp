Sr. PHP Developer (Start-Up) - New York, NY - Up to 120,000

A small, well-funded, rapidly growing company is looking to hire good people
who are good developers.

We're looking for a good senior level php/mysql developer to help us build a
high quality, extensible, and scalable codebase.

Qualified candidates will have at least 3-4 years of industry experience
managing web and interactive media development projects. Knowledge of PHP,
PERL HTML, JavaScript, MySQL, etc. are desired as is any general experience
with a wide range of web development technologies and frameworks. Previous
industry experience in Digital Media, Web Development/Design, or Interactive
Media is ideal. Candidates must have a Bachelors Degree.

The Senior Developer will be responsible for requirements gathering and
analysis, managing the entire project life cycle, design, documentation,
scheduling, and resource allocation. Candidates must demonstrate the ability
to manage multiple projects simultaneously and have the ability to
multi-task in a fast-paced environment. Applicants must have excellent
verbal and written communication skills.

DUTIES:

*	Participates in a team of (initially) four to six programmers
*	Programs in PHP/MYSQL and some PERL to maintain existing systems
such as CMS, Ad Server, Statistics reporting/logging systems, and client
reporting tools.
*	Responds to new feature requests maintaining key systems in
accordance with long-range software development roadmaps under strict
revision control
*	Assists with maintenance of several large networks of
GNU/Linux-based application, database, and development servers based on
open-source software
*	Updates job knowledge by keeping abreast of industry developments,
participating in educational opportunities, reading professional
publications, and maintaining personal networks
*	Documents software and systems

REQUIRED SKILLS:

*	PHP
*	Expertise in configuration and maintenance of MySQL
*	Perl
*	Expertise in configuration and maintenance of Apache web server
*	Significant Experience managing teams of developers.
*	Experience managing offsite development teams.
*	HTML/CSS (hand-coding in dynamic web programming applications only!)
*	XML
*	Experience with other scripting and programming languages (Bash,
Python, C, etc.) a plus!
*	Proficiency in *NIX command line and with common *NIX tools
*	Usage of Subversion/CVS in a complex development environment

*	Excellent verbal and written communication skills
*	Superior time management skills
*	Software development cycle fundamentals
*	Object-oriented design (OOD) experience
*	Software debugging
