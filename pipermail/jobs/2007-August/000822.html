COME WORK FOR INFORMATION SYSTEMS AT NYU'S COLLEGE OF DENTISTRY!

We have an immediate opportunity at the College of Dentistry for a skilled
Programmer / Analyst, Requisition #6293BR:

The Programmer will perform application and systems programming, 
including systems analysis. They will formulate and define scope and 
objectives of new and/or modified computer programs, including gathering 
and analyzing program requirements, preparing detailed program 
specifications, coding, testing, and maintaining those programs. The 
Programmer will supervise technical employees.

Bachelor's degree and 3 years' relevant experience with production 
scheduling and operations, systems analysis, client/server programming, 
or an equivalent combination of education and experience. Strong ability 
to establish priorities and resolve problems required. Excellent 
analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills needed. Must 
include a very good working knowledge of MYSQL, PHP, Apache, HTML, RH 
Linux, and some working knowledge of Oracle 8i/9i, Microsoft OS and 
applications.  An extensive knowledge of writing/maintaining/supporting 
LAMP applications is needed. Bachelor's degree in Information Systems, 
Linux Certification, SQL Reporting and Cold Fusion preferred.

For more information about working at NYU and to apply for this 
positions, visit our website at: www.nyu.edu/hr/jobs/apply , applying 
directly to Requisition 6293BR.

When asked how were you referred to NYU, please select the appropriate 
source from the drop-down menu. We accept online applications only.  NYU 
is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action Employer.
