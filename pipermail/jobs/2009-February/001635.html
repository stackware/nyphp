JR. WEB PROGRAMMER

Tisch School of the Arts, a division of New York University, currently  
has a FT opening for a PHP/Web Programmer. In this role, you will work  
on projects such as developing our new student/employer networking & e- 
Portfolio system, ongoing design enhancements to the main Tisch web  
site, developing new administrative and academic services  
applications.  Work in creative, multimedia rich environment in this  
preeminent school of performing & visual arts. Opportunity for  
professional growth. Excellent benefits including tuition remission, 5  
weeks vacation.

Qualifications:  A bachelor’s degree and at least 2 years’ relevant  
programming experience in a LAMP (Linux/Apache/MySQL/PHP) environment,  
or equivalent combination and a proficiency in HTML, CSS and  
JavaScript. Excellent analytical and communications skills with an  
ability to work independently, yet function well on a team. Must have  
experience in class-based/OO programming, and developing and  
supporting MySQL/SQL intra/Internet database systems.

Ideal candidates will have experience with PHP-based CMSs and an  
understanding of MVC frameworks. (Zend Framework a big plus.)  
Knowledge of XML/XSLT, AJAX, JQuery and PhotoShop/Illustrator helpful.

How to apply:

You must apply for this position online at:
http://nyu.edu/hr
Follow the navigation to Job Seekers>Apply For Jobs>Search Postings

Search for posting number:  20090136
Position Title: Web Programmer
