Type: Full-time Job
Location: New York, NY

Senior Developer / Analyst- PHP5
Marketing & Communications Digital Media
Reports to Director, Digital Media Marketing



Position Summary:
Develop, build and improve technical systems and applications, to ensure
efficiency, reliability and optimal performance of UJA-Federation of New
York's website. The ideal candidate is not only technically proficient
but is also passionate about evolving the organization's web
capabilities.

Major Responsibilities:
* Develop extensions/applications to the content management system (CMS)
SilverStripe and manage key functionality to meet stakeholders' needs
* Plan, analyze, design, implement and test systems and new
functionality
* Assist with the implementation of a digital asset management system
DMS and integration with the website CMS 
* Ongoing maintenance and troubleshooting of the CMS and DAM systems 
* Administering site for content development for non-programmers to
enable them to easily update content 
* Work with the Director to assure site is updated to current business
needs
* Other departmental web projects as necessary

Essential Technical Skills:
* Two or more years experience with PHP5 object-oriented programming
* Five or more years experience with general object-oriented programming
(PHP4, Java, .NET, Ruby, Python, and so forth
* Two or more years experience with model-view-controller (MVC)
development using SilverStripe Sapphire, Zend Framework, Symfony and so
forth
* Five or more years experience with database design, including advanced
knowledge of MySQL versions 4 and 5
* Proficient with XHTML/CSS template creation (standards compliant)
* Experienced with JavaScript libraries such as jQuery and Prototype
* Complete project life-cycle experience
* Report and documentation writing and research experience

Desirable Skills/Aptitudes:
* Familiarity with various open source programming languages and
mobile-phone development
* Experience working with content management systems
* Enthusiasm for the web as a medium and interest in emerging
technologies and trends in social media
* Strong organizational and communication skills, plus the ability to
manage multiple projects and prioritize competing tasks
* Ability to multitask a large number of projects simultaneously and
excellent problem solving skills
* Dynamic, results-orientated self-starter 
* Team player who can work with internal partners
* Ability to maintain a professional demeanor and sense of humor in a
high pressured environment
* Excellent Interpersonal skills

Education/Experience:
* BS in computer science or related field preferred
* Minimum 5+ years experience in related field

About the Organization 
The world's largest local philanthropy, UJA-Federation of New York cares
for those in need, rescues those in harm's way, and renews and
strengthens the Jewish people in New York, in Israel, and around the
world. Funds raised by UJA-Federation sustain the activities of more
than 100 health, human-service, educational, and community agencies.
Every day, these community-based organizations provide a multitude of
services that improve and enhance people's lives. For more information,
visit UJA-Federation's website at www.ujafedny.org
<http://www.ujafedny.org/> .
