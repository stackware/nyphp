Bandwidth Productions is a small Webby Award winning web
development/design firm located in Chelsea, New York City.  Part
established development company, part startup, we are financially stable
with long time clients, but also make time to work on our own projects.
We offer you the unique opportunity to work in an entrepreneurial/non
beauracractic environment for a company that has a consistent revenue
source.
 
We're looking for a web developer (LAMP) to play a strong role in our
continued growth. You will work with our existing team to develop custom
applications from start to finish for a variety of websites, many of
which are high profile. 
 
We offer a competitive salary, health/vision/dental benefits, regular
hours and profit sharing (after one year). This is an opportunity to
sink your teeth into a variety of projects in an entrepreneurial
environment. 
 
Your role: 

You will spend most of your time building and maintaining custom
applications for our clients. Applications range from full scale content
management systems to complex e-commerce applications and beyond. The
rest of your time will be spent developing new applications and features
for FIQL.com, a popular and growing music playlist social network.
FIQL.com is wholly owned, operated and funded by us. 
 
Who you are: 
 
A talented developer with 2-3 years experience in PHP, MySQL, Linux, and
Apache (LAMP). You are a creative thinker who is not afraid to challenge
us or be challenged. You are self-motivated and have experience working
with others, especially designers. 
 
The following skills are REQUIRED: 
 
1. Minimum of 2-3 solid years building complete applications in
PHP/MYSQL 
2. Proficiency in HTML, Javascript, DHTML/CSS 
3. Basic system administration skills:
. use of RPM or other package managers
. Experience in Pear package use and installation
. Familiarity with software firewalls, such as iptables
. Experience maintaining off-the-shelf PHP products such as PHPBB or
OpenAds (formerly phpAds)
 
The following skills are DESIRED: 
 
1. BS in Computer Science or related field 
2. Experience building and using social networking (Web 2.0)
applications
3. Experience building your own applications using third party APIs,
Facebook Platform and/or OpenSocial
 
How to apply: 
 
Contact: jobs at bandwidthproductions.com
 
Send resume with URLs and/or descriptions of relevant web applications
you've developed, describing your duties and responsibilities. 
 
About the company: 
 
Bandwidth Productions, Inc. is a small, very active web design and
development agency located in Chelsea, NY (26th and 7th). We provide
end-to-end web solutions from design to development to hosting. We also
produce FIQL.com, a popular music playlist social network wholly owned
and operated by us. We are small so decisions are made quickly. We have
been around since 2001 and have been profitable from day one.
