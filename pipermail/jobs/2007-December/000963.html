Code and Theory is hiring at all levels in both our New York and San  
Francisco offices.

Are you an expert coder with a great eye for UI? Do you enjoy making  
software that people not only interact with but experience? If you  
have a passion for usable, interactive, experiential software, then we  
are looking for you. We are looking for problem solvers who enjoy  
meeting new challenges head-on.

Code and Theory is looking for enthusiastic, detail oriented software  
engineers to join our team. As part of this team you will be  
contribute to architecting, building, and troubleshooting cutting-edge  
web applications and software. We need dedicated and meticulous  
engineers that take great pride in delivering only the highest quality  
work. As part of our team, you must be a fluent coder with a strong  
understanding of OOP and design patterns. You also must possess strong  
analytical and troubleshooting skills. Great communication with your  
fellow team members is essential.

This role requires:

• Expertise in coding and troubleshooting systems ranging from small,  
single server websites to large scale, distributed software systems.
• Experience architecting highly available web applications.
• Strong understanding of OOP and design patterns
• Willingness and efficiency learning new programming languages
• Meticulous attention to detail, strong sense of ownership and  
urgency, and a drive to over-deliver on projects

Qualifications:

• BA/BS in Computer Science or equivalent experience
• 3+ years experience developing interactive, dynamic, data-driven,  
highly available websites and software systems
• Expertise in one or more of the following: ActionScript 2/3, Java, C+ 
+, PHP
• Strong knowledge of XHTML, CSS, and JavaScript (DHTML, AJAX, table- 
less layouts are a plus)
• Working knowledge of the following is a plus: Perl, Python, Ruby on  
Rails
• Working knowledge of UNIX/Linux is a plus
• Working knowledge of SQL is a plus
• Experience leading and architecting projects is a plus
• A proven track record of delivering quality software projects on time
• Willingness and ability to handle requests outside of your core  
responsibilities and be team player

About CODE AND THEORY:

Code and Theory is a software design and development company based in  
lower Manhattan and San Francisco. We specialize in creating  
compelling online destinations and interactive experiences across  
multiple platforms including PC, TV, and mobile. Our work includes  
everything from immersive Flash environments and video game interfaces  
to large-scale community websites and video delivery systems. Visit http://codeandtheory.com
