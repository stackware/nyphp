90943BR Programmer/Analyst 
 
Employer: AOL, LLC.
 
Location: 75 Rockefeller Plaza; New York, NY 10019 US
 
Job Qualifications:
*Bright, savvy individual with a Bachelor's degree from a four-year
college.
*Must be a self-motivated problem solver who enjoys developing simple
solutions to complex problems.

Job Description:
The Marketing Communications department, a very lean team that is
responsible for producing and maintaining Platform-A's internal sales
communication site and external trade marketing site. The Marketing
Communications Programmer/Analyst will responsible for developing and
supporting innovative technical functionality for the internal website
(AOL/Sales) and other key communication platforms. This position will
report to Marketing Communications Technical Director.

Job Responsibilities:
The successful candidate for this position will focus on maintaining and
enhancing web, search, database and digital asset repository
functionality within the framework of the TYPO3 content management
system currently in production. This person will be involved at all
levels in the implementation of a given project including gathering
requirements, design, development, system integration, preparation of
test data, testing and debugging, preparation of documentation status
updates and training, when required. Performs related duties as assigned
or requested.

This candidate will need a Bachelors degree with 3-5 years of
programming experience in PHP, Perl, SQL and Unix Shell. This position
is about working as part of a small team to solve problems so the ideal
candidate will be able to work collaboratively, identify areas of
improvement, generate and implement creative technical solutions and get
results.

Required Skills and Experience:
Bachelors Degree.
3-5 years technical experience.
Programming Experience in at OO PHP, Perl, Javascript, SQL ( MySQL 5 ) 
Shell(sh/csh/ksh), Perl, C.
HTML, CSS
Strong problem solving skills.

Desired Skills and Experience:
Database design experience in MySQL5.
Programming experience within the Typo3 cms environment
 
To apply: Send resume to john.bentley at corp.aol.com  with " 90943BR
Programmer/Analyst" as the title of your email.
 
 
John Bentley
Manager, Talent Acquisition
