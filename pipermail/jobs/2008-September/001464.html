Net at Work is currently seeking web-designers to design a range of sites, varying from Flash to full e-commerce systems. Applicants must have experience building dynamic database driven sites, navigation design and usability theory, working with developers as well as designing and building templates. 

Applicants should be primarily focused on the web, with at least three years industry experience building sites as a designer or lead designer (not a contributor). Print and motion graphics work is a plus but is not our core focus. Applicants should have a basic grounding in typography and design principals but should also love the web, including its restrictions. 

Applicants also need to have a strong knowledge of standards compliant (X)HMTL and CSS (Javascript and Actionscript 3 a plus and, of course, some PHP). You should also be painfully familiar with resolution issues, browser testing, browser hacks etc.
