ID Society, Inc. is seeking a highly motivated Senior Technical  
Developer with previous professional experience. The Senior Technical  
Developer will plan and implement solutions leveraging Internet  
technologies for clients and internal projects.

Qualified candidate is required to work on-site at our New York City  
office. Only local applicants, please. This is a full-time position.


REQUIRED Skills

- Minimum five years of recent professional experience
- Required Competencies: PHP, SQL(MySQL), Flash, XML, XHTML,  
JavaScript, CSS, CVS, UNIX
- Demonstrated expertise in developing and deploying client/server  
applications
- Experience in application design, programming architecture, and  
software lifecycle
- E-commerce experience
- Object-oriented programming
- Relational database design
- Standards-compliant web authoring techniques
- Strong communication and technical writing skills
- Exceptional problem solving skills a must


BENEFICIAL Skills

- Experience with Open Source CMS such as Joomla, Mambo, WordPress etc.
- Microsoft-centric technologies and languages (such as ASP and .Net)
- Additional object-oriented programming languages (such as Java, C++)
- Enterprise-level RDBMS software
- Experience with optimizing large databases
- Professional Systems Administration experience in Unix or Windows
- Quality Assurance testing


COMPANY

Founded in 1998, we are an interactive marketing agency that helps  
clients extend their brands online to reach more customers. Through  
the combination of strategic thinking and award-winning creative, our  
products and services are designed to acquire, engage, and retain new  
customers by increasing value to their brand.

As experts in technology and marketing, our services work to solve  
clients' business problems and achieve measurable results. We execute  
and deliver through relationship marketing services (customer  
acquisition and retention programs), creative executions (Web site  
design, online video, and email and newsletter campaigns) and  
strategic marketing consulting (product branding, positioning, and  
sales enhancement programs).

Our clients include prestigious firms such as L'Oreal, SKYY Spirits,  
CondéNet and ABC Family Network. For more information, visit  
www.idsociety.com.


HOW TO APPLY (If you do not include ALL of the information below,  
your resume will NOT be considered.)

Please apply by sending the following materials to jobs- 
tech at idsociety.com:

- Cover letter and current resume
- 2-4 examples of work that best represents your abilities, along  
with a description of the role you played for each project.
- Salary requirements
