Senior PHP Developer | New York, NY | 85-100k

Job description:
My client is a dynamic, cutting-edge media company that specializes in
up-to-the-minute publishing of travel deals, destination information,
and advice. We are expanding rapidly as millions of readers drive growth
for our website, weekly e-newsletters, and forthcoming magazine.

My client offers has the casual work environment of a small company, but
has the opportunities and benefits only available in larger media
conglomerates * including health insurance, 401K, and 3 weeks paid
vacation per year! They are seeking driven, hard-working, and
career-oriented individuals to join their winning team. 

The position is in a fast, energetic environment working
interdepartmentally with our marketing team, editors, and ad sales
department. It requires in-depth knowledge of both front-end and
back-end technologies. You will be intimately involved with defining
processes and must be able to work collaboratively with others during
all stages of the development cycle. 

REQUIRED SKILLS: 
1) Advanced knowledge of PHP 5, specifically OO aspects of the language.
2) Experience with Ruby on Rails is a plus. 
3) Experience with Linux and Apache. 
4) Expert understanding of JavaScript, HTML, and the DOM (AJAX). 
5) Expert understanding of object oriented design. 
6) Experience with N-Tier Architecture. 
7) Experience with both MySQL and MS SQL Server. DBA skills highly
desired. 
8) Love of open source development and standards-based development. 
9) Internationalization experience is a plus. 
10) Strong communication skills. 
11) Able to multi-task and project manage well. 

If you are interested in this opportunity and are a tri-state (NY, NJ,
CT) resident and US Citizen or green card holder, please submit your
resume, salary requirements, and a paragraph (or two) highlighting your
skills/experience as it pertains to this job to
mike at open-source-staffing.com
