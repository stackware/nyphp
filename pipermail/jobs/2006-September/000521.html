Community Connect Inc. is seeking experienced Senior PHP Developers to
join the Development team in our Manhattan office!

 

COMPANY PROFILE:

With more than 28 million registered members and 30 million dynamic page

most heavily trafficked sites in the world. Our technologists are the
leading experts in developing high performing, scalable clustered web
applications like BlackPlanet.com, AsianAvenue.com and Migente.com

 

QUALIFICATION & SKILLS:

Required:

*	Strong passion for technology and an urge to learn and get
better everyday.
*	5+ years of experience including analysis, design, coding, and
implementation of object-oriented Web applications.
*	4+ years of development experience with Oracle or another major
RDBMS.
*	Strong understanding and deep experience in working with
database-driven websites using dynamic languages like PHP, Perl, Java,
JSP, C, ASP, etc.
*	BS degree (in Computer Science or related discipline)

Preferred:

*	Knowledge of Agile Methodologies, such as Extreme Programming
(XP) and Scrum.
*	Past experience with test-driven development, refactoring.
*	Experience leading and mentoring individuals and teams.
*	Knowledge of a UNIX type operating system (preferably Linux)
*	Previous contributions to open source projects.
