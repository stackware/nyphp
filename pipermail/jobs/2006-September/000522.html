Project Manager   (For software development life cycles)

 

IndustryNext is a technology and design firm delivering innovative
products and services to clients and consumers. The interactive consumer
experiences we have created are being used by millions of people. 

These experiences leverage and run on the latest devices, technologies,
frameworks, design patterns, interfaces and platforms.  If you are like
us; passionate about the digital medium and enjoy the challenge of using
technology and design to create compelling experiences you might want to
join us. 

The successful candidate will have: 

* 5+ years experience as a project manager in the software/web
development industry 
* Lead the successful delivery of client and company projects 
* Be responsible for the on-time, within budget delivery of custom
development projects from requirements definition phase through testing
and rollout 
* Understand all technical design principles as well as the creative
process 
* Develop comprehensive product requirement documents, plans and
roadmaps 


* Ensure that project implementation is consistent with company and
client requirements 
* Develop strategies to help achieve company objectives 
* Identify and mitigate risks and offer solutions 
* Document all communications, status reports, requirements & designs 
* Communicate necessary information to team members, management, and
clients 


* Work with all areas of the company to ensure readiness for project
delivery 
* Establish and maintain relationships with staff members of all levels
and in all departments of the company 
* Conduct meetings with the project team and drive the agenda and
resolutions 
* Prepare and run project reviews and take action on improvement areas 


* Manage project time tracking for each engineer and designer 
* Allocate resources effectively to maintain project level and company
wide profitability 
* Ability to manage scope properly and maintain strong client
relationships 
* Strong consulting craft and client side skills preferred 
* Experience with P&L preferred

This person will have strong leadership skills and ability to interact
with all employees and management and foster cross-functional teamwork
among all departments and companies. Other qualities highly recommended
are: Flexibility and willingness to adapt to individual project
requirements; effective written, verbal, presentation and communication
skills; strong technical skills with an ability to quickly assimilate
new ideas and concepts; highly skilled at multi-tasking in a rapidly
paced environment; proven track record of meeting deadlines;
understanding of the software development life cycle. This individual
should have a minimum of five years experience successfully managing
multiple projects on-time in a software development or technology
oriented company. A Bachelors degree is required.
