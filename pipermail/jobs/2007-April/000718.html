UDFI is a dynamic, cutting-edge technology firm that specializes in working
with businesses and their web technologies. We are expanding rapidly as the
web becomes integrated into everyday life. Our core principles are what
define us: Usability, Design, Focus and Innovation. It's a casual but fast
paced work environment and we are constantly challenged with interesting
projects.

We are looking for a developer with both PHP and Coldfusion development
experience.  This specific project is working with a large software
development firm on the west coast that has integrated the web into the
latest version of their software. You'll be specifically working with
several teams from some of the biggest names out there, doing Coldfusion
development support for the current code base and for future enhancements.
This position will offer you exposure to cutting edge technology such as the
Apollo framework and Coldfusion Scorpion.

In your downtime you'll be working with PHP to develop our soon to be
launched SMS product which will revolutionize the way people and companies
use text-messaging.  You'll be on the ground floor of something special!

This position will be a great addition to your resume as you would be
working with some of the worlds most influential companies in the world on
design, as well as some cutting edge Object Oriented application
development.

If you're eager to learn, this is the job for you!  We're here to shepherd
your career while maximizing your exposure to the latest and greatest
technologies.

Requirements (you will be tested):
* Good work ethic and communication
* 1 - 3 years of Coldfusion MX7
* 1 - 3 years of PHP
* Good understanding of Object Oriented development
* MySQL - Standard Queries, Stored Procs, Indexes

Nice To haves:
* AJAX
* Symfony framework experience
* XML / XSLT
* Flash

Please send a cover letter and your resume and expect a call back from us
today or tomorrow!

All the best,
David Wang
Founder, Technical Project Manager
UDFI, LLC.
