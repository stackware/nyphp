Job opening: Web Developer
Email resumes to: wire-webdev at limewire.com

Lime Wire LLC, maker of the famous file-sharing program, seeks an
exceptional Web Developer. The right candidate will be a high-energy
individual, open to new ideas, self-motivated, a quick study, and willing to
develop new skills, while constantly improving upon existing abilities.
Candidates must also be a strong multi-tasker with exceptional
time-management skills.

As a Web Developer, you will design and code the back-end of Lime Wire's
websites: www.limewire.org and www.limewire.com. Ideal candidates must have
a strong background in web development--from databases all the way to
front-end HTML/CSS. Familiarity with web applications, architectures, and
deployment is also necessary. Responsibilities include programming and
modifying the PHP code of MediaWiki, WordPress, and vBulletin.

Qualifications
Required:
*Copious experience with SQL, Perl, and PHP 
*Familiarity with PHP web applications, namely MediaWiki, Wordpress,
vBulletin

Bonus:
*Understanding of object-oriented programming and MVC architectures
*Computer science background/degree

MORE ABOUT US
We've been in business since September 2000, and we're continuing to grow.
We have new projects on the horizon and need smart, innovative people to
join our team. We pride ourselves on maintaining a happy, healthy work
environment where a free exchange of ideas is encouraged. Lime Wire's
offices are located in Tribeca in downtown Manhattan (no stuffy cubicles).
We offer competitive salaries; excellent benefits, including 5 weeks of
vacation per year; and great perks, including free lunches, snacks, drinks,
trips to Shakespeare in the Park, company-sponsored trips to locales like
Whistler, Puerto Rico and Costa Rica, fabulous parties, and just great
company. Our workplace is casual and while we work hard, we always have a
little time for a game of poker or a summer party on our garden roof deck.

Thanks!
Luck Dookchitra
Lime Wire LLC
