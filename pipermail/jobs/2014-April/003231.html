Developer Tech Support / Jr Devops (will train)!! 

NYC - Digital Media Company

 

We are looking for an enthusiastic recent college graduate or someone with
1-2 years experience to join the devops team.  You will be trained!!  High
growth potential in this position.  

You will start out supporting the development team of about 20 with
troubleshooting etc.   You will quickly learn the technical environment and
skills necessary to become a full member of the devops team.

 

We will invest in you!  Apply!  

 

About You 

.   You are enthusiastic about learning new skills as quickly as possible.  

.   You may know how to script but would like to improve.  

.   Know a bit about Linux and we will teach you the rest

.   Familiar about networking infrastructure and never hesitant to learn
more.  

.   We will teach you to use AWS services .

.    Basic PHP skills or knowledge

.   Basic scripting capabilities (e.g. Python, Bash, or Ruby)

.   A passion to learn, improve, and implement services within the cloud

.   Excellent communication and people skills (we are a fun bunch to work
with and want you to be fun too)

.   Able to multi-task and quickly adapt to new requirements

.   Able to have fun working in a tight-knit, agile development group

.   Computer Science or equivalent degree

 

Nice to Have

.   Experience in one or more programming languages 

.   Hands on experience with AWS Services

.   Some very basic knowledge in Networking and Monitoring Services

.   Familiar with deploying services such as Hadoop, RabbitMQ, Redis, or
MySQL

.   Knowledge of Web Frameworks

.   Knowledge in a provisioning or automation framework
