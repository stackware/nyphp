social tagging software. We desperately need a new public face for our 
website, and we’re seeking an experienced Joomla! person to help us with 
setup and templates.

That means we’re looking for someone to work with us starting this

pay realistic rates, especially for top-notch, reliable consultants who 
can commit to making our strict deadline on such short notice. If you

rates and contact info. We need to find someone this weekend, so don’t 
delay!
