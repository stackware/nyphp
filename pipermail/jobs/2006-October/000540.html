IndustryNext is a technology and design firm delivering innovative

>From 3D applications for Windows Media Center in Vista, to Set-Top box

environment.  Our projects are challenging, cutting edge, and extremely
high profile.

projects.  

 

If you have a passion for innovation at the very forefront of the tech
industry and enjoy a nimble startup-like environment, we would love to
hear from you.

 

Requirements:

*	Intense passion and curiosity for the latest technologies
*	A creative and curious mind
*	Ideas and a point of view on what's next and your part in it
