TANDEMSEVEN JOB DESCRIPTION

 

SENIOR FRONT-END WEB DEVELOPERs with AJAX, JavaScript, HTML, CSS and
Rich Internet Toolkits

 

Position Locations: NYC

 

Please forward resumes in Word format to:  ilevitt at tandemseven.com

 

Job Title:  SENIOR FRONT-END WEB DEVELOPERS WITH AJAX, JavaScript, HTML,
CSS and Rich Internet Toolkits

 

TandemSeven is a firm that designs and architects business portals and
ebusiness Applications specializing in creating usable, intuitive
interfaces for complex applications.  

 

POSITION DESCRIPTION:

The Senior Front-End Web Developers position requires candidates that
are skilled in developing technical solutions, but also has strong
experience working with clients. While we prefer an unrestricted ability
to travel, TandemSeven would like to anchor technical teams around in
the Boston, Chicago and New York areas.

 

Qualifications:

*	4+ years JavaScript/HTML/CSS ("AJAX") experience. 
*	Familiar with rich internet toolkits such as Dojo, Zapatec,
jQuery, Tibco GI, Backbase, or others.
*	Experience with server-side web frameworks such as JSP or
ASP.Net.
*	Expert HTML, JavaScript, and CSS Skills.
*	Experience with XML and XSLT
*	Experience with UI frameworks such as XUL, Flex, and XAML a plus
*	Must be able to work in the United States and travel to customer
engagements.  Ability to travel approximately 50% (2-3 evenings a week)
*	Experience working with a design team 
*	Minimum of at least 3 years working experience with at least 1
year of technical consulting 
*	Financial and/or Portal experience is a plus
*	At least a BS or BCS is required

 

As part of TandemSeven's standard employment process, candidates will
undergo a complete background check prior to an offer being extended.
These background checks are conducted by a professional third party firm
at no charge to the applicant and include:  prior employment
verification, education verification, social security trace, criminal
background check and motor vehicles records (where required for
position).

 

TandemSeven Supports Workforce Diversity
