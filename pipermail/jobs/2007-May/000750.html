Want to work on a product? Want to work on a site with direct interaction
with its end users? Want to work in a creative environment where you have
influence on new functionality and features? Do you like doing R&D on new
and upcoming tech? Read on ...

Massify is hiring a Mid to Senior Level PHP Developer.

We are looking for a dynamic, multiple-hat wearing, enthusiastic mid- to
senior level PHP developer interested in working in an evolving,
cross-disciplined environment.

You, our ideal candidate, have a mind for creative problem solving and 3 to
5 years experience working on production websites with PHP using MVC
patterns.

You appreciate the beauty of the command line and know what third normal
form is. You want to be a part of something creative and work in a fun
environment.

About Us

Massify is a collaborative, film grant, social networking site for film
creatives and enthusiasts.

We are creating a unique web space where users can develop story ideas and
compete for film grants. It's a place where people pitch movie/story ideas,
critique pitches, connect with each other and win filmmaking grants.

Here's more about the job:

Requirements:

* 3-5 years development experience on commercial production sites
* OOP PHP experience.
* CSS/HTML/JavaScript skills
* Administration of RDBMS systems
* Perl experience

Pluses:

* *NIX Administration - Apache configuration, shell scripting, DNS
configuration
* Experience optimizing and tuning apache and php
* Likes AJAX, but understands its place

Responsibilities

* Support and advise Director of Technology in all areas of development
* Participate in creative process - design through deployment
* R&D new technologies for future site features

This position is full time and on-site in New York. The position is open to
U.S. residents with authorization to work. We prefer not to relocate, but
for you, our ideal candidate, anything is possible.

Massify offers a competitive salary, Apple or Linux workstations, and the
chance to be a part of a team that's making something massif-ly cool.

Please send your CV/Resumes to jobs|at|massifycorp.com
