We are a leading provider of transaction-based rewards and offer programs for
national banks, insurance companies and magazines, located in midtown Manhattan.

Our fast-growing company with major clients in this exploding and innovative industry has
openings for full-time, experienced Developers and Engineers to work on a variety of projects.

Learn our proprietary MVC framework and CMS, and create functional pages for both administrative control and personalized end-user interaction on a robust platform serving tens of millions of users.

We are also developing unique applications to perform "Big Data" analytics and provide insights and visualizations on various types of profiling, reporting and campaign related activity.

These are career-growth positions for the right individuals; join our team of programmers and engineers and you will be exposed to a variety of
applications with the opportunity to start in - or "learn up" into - a system-owner / manager role.  Your appetite for information and attention to
detail will allow you to master the existing code base and learn our complex business. Being well-rounded, with communication
and project management skills, will make you even more valuable.

As a vendor serving Financial Institutions, security is paramount.  Familiarity with OWASP guidelines and experience with all aspects web security is a plus.  Background checks are required.

Experience with WAP and/or mobile is also a plus.

SKILLS REQUIRED
- Detail Oriented
- LAMP stack experience
- Good Communication skills
- Excellent Analytical skills

PROGRAMMING - 3-4 years experience
- write clean, readable code
- adhere to company standards and security guideline
- understand existing code base and improve/enhance, don't re-create
- provide insights and feedback for architecture and product

ENGINEERING - 5-10 years experience
- Everything under Programming (above) plus...
- deep understanding of entire application
- understanding of all elements of the stack and their interactions to track down performance issues
- strong trouble-shooting skills
- implement specialized technologies such as SAML, encryption, APIs, etc
- work with clients for API and Web Service integrations
- strong database skills to separate data issues from code issues

TECHNICAL MANAGMENT 10+ years experience
- Everything under Engineering (above) plus...
- Strong verbal and written communication skills
- Desire to build a career with a growing company - no job shoppers.
- Ability to partner and work in a fast-paced, collaborative team environment
- Ability to manage multiple projects and delegate tasks
- Sense of humor

GROWTH OPPORTUNITIES
  Learn existing, proprietary systems and interface with Program Managers on requirements and scoping.
  Create sophisticated data management systems on a patent-pending platform.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  Linux / PHP / PostgreSQL / HTML / CSS / Javascript / jQuery

ADDITIONAL TECHNOLOGIES:
  Expert Linux / Hadoop / SAML / DNS / SMTP / Mobile / Networks

QUALIFICATIONS:
   Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science or related discipline

We offer a competitive salary with full benefits in an exciting industry, working
with a talented group in a centrally located midtown office.

Salary will be commensurate with experience, and also based on organizational and cultural fit.
