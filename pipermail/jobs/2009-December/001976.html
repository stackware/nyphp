SUMMARY:
The Software Engineer of Managed Solutions is responsible for designing, 
developing, writing, implementing, and maintaining various web based 
applications. Duties include requirement analysis, design, implementation, 
testing, integration and maintenance of applications to meet strategic 
business goals and client needs.

REQUIREMENTS:
- Capable of reviewing, analyzing and modifying applications including 
coding, testing, debugging, and documenting applications
- Must be able to troubleshoot issues and work with the appropriate 
resources/parties to resolve them
- Must have knowledge of a variety of concepts, practices, and procedures 
in development
- A wide range of discretion and judgment is expected of the candidate in 
order to work independently to plan and accomplish goals
- Must also possess a high degree of creativity and a strong talent for 
problem-solving to conquer complex technical obstacles
- Strong understanding of web technologies and web design
- Ability to hand-code HTML and Advanced JavaScript
- Awareness of cross-browser and cross-platform compatibility issues
- A proven desire to learn and contribute to the successful completion of 
team goals and projects
 
ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS / RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Highly accomplished, front-end web developer to produce HTML/CSS-styled 
web based applications
- Responsibilities will include HTML/CSS/JavaScript/PHP/XML/JSP coding
- Build test plans to ensure code modifications do not result in failures 
of project specifications
- Ensure that deployed applications are properly maintained throughout 
their lifecycle
 
REQUIRED DEGREE AND/OR EXPERIENCE:
Bachelor's Degree or equivalent in Computer Science or related field
Professional experience (or related academic experience) working with PHP, 
HTML/CSS, JavaScript, XML, Java/JSP
 
PREFERRED DEGREE AND/OR EXPERIENCE:
Familiarity with common developer tools (Ant, CVS/SVN, etc)
Familiarity with PHP frameworks (Zend, CodeIgniter, Kohana, CakePHP, or 
Symfony)
Experience with AJAX and JavaScript libraries (jQuery, Prototype)
Experience with financial data is a plus
Experience developing database-driven/XML-based Flash/Flex components is a 
plus
Experience in TCL is a huge plus

This is a full-time position located in New York City.  Please submit your 
resume to jim.yi at interactivedata.com.
