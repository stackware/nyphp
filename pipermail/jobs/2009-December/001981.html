  Position Title: PHP Developer - Stamford, CT 

  To Apply:

  Overview:
Kodak is the market leader in providing photographic products and services 
to consumers and commercial customers around the globe for memories, 
entertainment and information. We bring innovative products to the market 
that allow people to take, make and share pictures and experience the 
magic of photography. As the world leader in imaging, Kodak is always 
looking for talented people. We work hard and have fun. We expect our 
future team members to be competent and driven. Our team is headed by a 
dynamic management team who are focused on results in conjunction with 
mentoring the development of each team member.

Our work environment is a fast-paced, open minded with tight deliverables 
and encourages the sharing of ideas and experiences to help the team 
excel. We offer competitive salaries, generous benefits packages and are 
located within a short walk from the Stamford train station and downtown 
Stamford.

Job Description and Qualifications Required: 

Our PHP Developers work in our fast-paced, Windows-based environment with 
tight deliverables. Ideal candidates should have analysis, design, 
deployment and optimization experience in both database-driven and 
web-based applications.

Candidates must be team players and able to work independently without 
requiring micromanagement. Good communication skills and ability to listen 
are also required.

Candidates must possess the following skills:

Practicing developer with 2-5+ years experience
PHP (4.3 / 5.0)
Strong SQL skills, mainly on Oracle RDB 
Oracle Stored Procedures, Functions & Triggers 
HTML/DHTML/JavaSrcipt/CSS

Desired skills include:

Java EE 5 experience (with EJB3.0)
Apache Web Server
.NET experience
MS SQL Server
Familiarity with the following technologies would be advantageous: Spring, 
JMS, XQuery, Glassfish, NetBeans IDE, jUnit

Educational Requirements:

Undergraduate degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, or related 
discipline is strongly preferred. Equivalent work experience is required. 
Graduate Degree a plus.
