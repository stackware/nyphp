TargetSpot (www.targetspot.com), an innovative advertising network for
streaming radio, is seeking an experienced PHP/LAMP Developer to join its
team and help us deliver a best-in-class advertising platform.

Requirements:

    * 2-3 years of proven proficiency in application development in a LAMP
environment (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP)
    * Solid understanding of application architecture and database design.
    * Ability to follow detailed functional specifications as well as act
independently.
    * Experience with Unit Testing your code.
    * Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or equivalent professional
experience.


The ideal Candidate will be:

    * Seasoned: able to tackle all sorts of programming projects from major
application features to internal admin tools.
    * Organized: able to quickly shift attention between fixing bugs and
writing code for new features without losing focus.
    * Proactive: able to identify potential problems in advance, and not be
afraid to voice your concerns.
    * Tenacious: must love writing solid code!


The position will be full-time for the right candidate.


Please send your resume (MS Word, PDF, or web link) to
targetspotjobs at gmail.com for consideration with the subject line: LAMP
Developer
