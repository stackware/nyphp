JOB DESCRIPTION:
Rapidly expanding e commerce biz is expanding in-house IT dept. We  
are a young, growing company in the Custom Window Treatment Business  
and are seeking an experienced and talented Software Developer with  
strong LAMP (Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP5) and E-Commerce  
development skills. Using these technologies you will be building new  
features as well as adopting existing features to suit our changing  
needs. Our fast-paced environment requires the ability to  
troubleshoot and react to any problems that may arise on our site. A  
full understanding of object-oriented PHP coding is a must. Projects  
include working on the development and maintenance of the online  
website as well as upgrading and improving the internal  
administration website. You will be working in a small, friendly  
environment which feels very "start-up", working side by side the  
Director of Technology and founding partners. Our office is located  
in Long Island City and is within walking distance to the 7 train  
line (just minutes out of midtown).

QUALIFICATIONS:
~ Must be a self-motivated, eager to get to work
~ Experienced working in start-ups, and the fires that need to be put  
out
~ Excellent communication and organizational skills
~ Comfortable balancing projects and priorities while consistently  
meeting project deadlines
~ 3- 5 years experience in PHP development, with extensive object- 
oriented skills

We don't care where you went to school, or what your degree was in.  
If you are the right candidate for this position, your skills will  
shine through. Applications are being considered for immediate hire.  
If you feel like you fit the bill, please send us your resume and  
include your salary requirements. Decisions will be made fairly  
quickly, so if you are questioning whether or not you are qualified  
for this position, please don't bother applying. We don't have time  
to waste, and are looking for talented individuals who are confident  
in their abilities and are looking to grow with us.

*** THIS IS AN ON-SITE POSITION ----- IF YOU DO NOT WANT TO WORK ON- 
SITE DO NOT REPLY. WE WILL NOT RESPOND ****

If qualified and interested in this position, please send your resume  
and salary requirements directly to me at nestor at theshadestore.com.
