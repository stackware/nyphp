Web Development Assistant

DesignAttic Multimedia, Inc. (www.designattic.com) is seeking a  
versatile assistant web developer familiar with PHP & MySQL, XHTML,  
and CSS to join our team and work on-site in our Long Island City  
location.  Candidate must have a portfolio that demonstrates basic  
design and layout sensibilities.  Familiarity with AJAX programming  
techniques and other Web 2.0 technologies is a plus.  Knowledge of  
Flash & ActionScript is an even bigger plus.

The right candidate will work directly with our graphic designers,  
interactive developers, and project coordinators.  Work schedule is  
flexible, and can include evenings and weekends.
All candidates must be able to commit 30 hours per week on-site.   
This is an entry-level position with room for growth as our company  
expands.

To find out more about DesignAttic Multimedia, please visit  
www.designattic.com

Rate: $8-10/hour depending on experience.

Please email all resumes in a PDF file to jobs at designattic.com in  
addition to some links of your work in the email.

Thanks!
