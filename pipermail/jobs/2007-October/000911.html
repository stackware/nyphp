PHP/MySQL developers.

community required)
* Unix/Linux, Apache, server administration knowledge a plus
* Knowledge of AJAX, JavaScript, XML a plus
* Experience creating client documentation (requirements, scope of work, 
etc.)
* Superior written and verbal communication skills
* Client relations skills


Please submit resumes, cover letter, salary requirements and code 
samples to jobs at achieveinternet.com.
