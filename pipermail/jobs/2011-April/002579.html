Technical Lead to assist in the definition, development and ongoing
support of our client's interactive and digital projects. 

The ideal candidate will be energetic, articulate, and must possess an

Requirements:

 

Hands-on experience with PHP and open-source server-side frameworks and
CMSs 

Expertise in HTML, CSS, JS and AS 

Proficiency with JS frameworks 

Experience integrating 3rd party API's and AJAX & JSONP widgets 

Experience in planning and developing web apps with extensive
server-side logic.

Continuous research of applicable technologies, platforms, toolsets, and
