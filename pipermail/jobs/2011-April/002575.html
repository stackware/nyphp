Hi,

I am hoping you could help me with a programmer need.  The position is with a major agency in New York City and the requirements description is:

LAMP Developer
Advanced PHP
Advanced MySQL
Advanced SVN and/or Git, including installation
Comfortable configuring server applications such as Apache, PHP, MySQL, FTP, Postfix, etc.
Comfortable administering users and file access / permissions on Red Hat
Comfortable setting-up rewrite rules for clean URLs
Familiarity with HTML, CSS, Javscript (specifically jQuery)

You will be joining a staff Front-End Developer. There are two big projects under development that need support. The first is an internal time tracking tool. Quite a bit of work has been done already. We want to bring someone on who is great with PHP and handwritten MySQL queries to finish writing "the brains" of the application so our Front End guy can skin it.

The second project that is just kicking off is our new website. All of the creative is done, and the HTML pages have begun to be laid-out. We are looking to bring on a heavy-hitting LAMP person right at the beginning. You will be tasked with: configuring the Apache server, setting-up the MySQL database, installing version control (SVN or Git), installing Wordpress, setting-up Rewrite rules, recommending a solution for the "Work" and "People" pages (possible custom CMS development), any necessary back-end development.

Length of time would be about 4 weeks. Location is NYC. Must be on-site.

Please let me know if you have any questions,
