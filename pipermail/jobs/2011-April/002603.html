As a "lame recruiter" let me remind you that U.S. Labor Law (State and
Federal) is very clear on the mention or implied metion of alcohol,
tobacco and other narcotics in job postings and job incentives. It is
not only prohibited, it also falls under discrimination of the equal
employment act (Title VII, the ADA) - this is not limited to job
advertisements; recruitment; testing; use of company facilities; et
al.  I urge you to be careful when posting mention of this - all it
takes is one complaint.
