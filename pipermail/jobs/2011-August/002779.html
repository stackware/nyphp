KeyWifi, a social entrepreneurial startup in the peer-to-peer sharing
space, is looking for a Chief Technical Officer to lead the next phase
of our implementation. Early feedback points to widespread demand for
KeyWifi’s solution, which delivers affordable internet and wifi
roaming: as a result, we are actively seeking a lead technical
resource who can prepare us for the oncoming avalanche of growth.

The leader we need is an innovator and unconventional thinker, someone
ready to challenge long-entrenched powers in the internet services
market. You should be conversant in online security and communication
protocols, notably RADIUS and 802.1x implementations; and with
extensive hands-on experience building user-friendly, reliable
consumer-facing sites. You should be comfortable with implementing
search-engine optimization and payment-system integrations. We built
our current system in Rails but are looking to you to set standards
and architectural direction.

All startups are risky, and we are ready to take a bet on you and your
readiness to take on new responsibility: we will consider both
seasoned CTOs, looking for the opportunity to “make a difference” in
an exciting startup committed to social change, and also candidates
for whom CTO is a step up in responsibility. PHP is an obvious way
forward both for its scalability and the depth of human resources
available.

As a bootstrapping startup actively raising investment, your main
compensation will be equity until our first round closes. At that time
we expect to pay a salary commensurate with your experience and
potential. We are based in New York, NY and while we prefer candidates
based here, we will consider working with you virtually if the fit is
right.

Please check out www.KeyWifi.com and drop us a line at cto at keywifi.com.
