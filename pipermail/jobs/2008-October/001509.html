Portero Inc is looking for a Senior PHP Developer

Needs
Minimum 6 years web development experience.
Minimum 4 years PHP development experience.
Extensive SQL (MySQL) knowledge.
Experience with revision control systems: SVN, SVK or Git.
Data Modeling experience.
E-commerce experience.
Experience working in a TDD environment.
Experience working with unit testing (SimpleTest).

Can code without depending on an IDE.

A love for documentation.
Experience developing high-traffic sites.
Advanced Bash or other scripting skills.
Experience with Memcache and MogileFS.

Experience with the following patterns & philosophies:
M-V-C pattern
ORM
Test Driven Development
Agile Development
DRY programming

Provides...
Flexible work schedule
Competitive salary based on experience and pertinent skills
Paid time off

A stable and exciting environment
