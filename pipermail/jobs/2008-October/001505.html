Full time hire in New York City


 


Summary Description  


The Director, Product Development is responsible for definition,
planning, and release of new and existing products. Responsible for
product planning and execution throughout the product lifecycle,
including: gathering and prioritizing product requirements, defining the
product vision, and working closely with services, technology, and
marketing to ensure revenue and customer satisfaction goals are met.
Work with end users and the technology team to translate business needs
into functional requirements for development, and to execute training
once a product is released. 

 


Responsibilities


*         Work with the Technology, Marketing, and Services on all
aspects of the Product Development lifecycle.   

*	Create strategy for product proposals and plans for new
products.
*	Manage detailed product requirement specifications for hand-off
to the technology team.

*	Define enhancements to existing products and planning of new
products. 

*	Provide product training.
*	Day-to-day management of team, hiring, and performance
management.

 


Required Skills/Experience


*	Define the product strategy and roadmap working with Product
Management.
*	Manage the prioritization process with senior management.

*         BS degree in computer-oriented technical discipline or
equivalent experience.

*	7+ years experience in Product Development, preferably with
e-commerce applications or the on-line marketing applications
marketplace.
*	Demonstrated success in product launches and end-to-end
execution of product development and lifecycle management.
*	Demonstrated ability to meet or beat assigned targets and
deadlines consistently.

*         Strong communication (oral/written) and analytical skills, as
well as a track record in technology requirements specification and
product development. 

*	Experience with Unified Modeling Language (UML) including use
cases. 
*	 Excellent oral & written communication skills.

*         Must have strong technical background in a business context.

*         Strong Microsoft Office skills (Word, Excel, PowerPoint and
Project).

*         Knowledge of the Application Service Provider industry.  

*         Solid understanding of on-line marketing and affiliate
marketing.

*         Familiarity with decision support, analytical applications, or
CRM a plus.
