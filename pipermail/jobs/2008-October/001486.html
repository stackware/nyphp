POSITION 1

Full time hire in Elkridge, MD-( south of Baltimore, MD)

Job Function

Lotame is developing the next generation of internet marketing
technology specifically geared towards social networking websites such
as MySpace and Facebook and is looking for a candidate who can server in
a dual role as a Network Engineer/System Administrator. The candidate
will help manage our local and production systems. Candidates for this
position will be knowledgeable in all things Linux with familiarity
working with Apache and MySQL and other assorted Open Source Software
packages. In addition, they must be comfortable in all of the
technological/network underpinnings that make a high traffic volume
Internet facing site tick, such as load balancing, firewalls, and DNS. 

 

The Day to Day

*         Administer a co-located network environment supporting web
services provided by the company 

*         Candidate must be able/willing to cross over between NE
(network) and SA (system admin) work 

*         Optimize server and application configurations to achieve the
best practical performance of the system 

*         Respond to and resolve system/network outages and performance
degradation issues 

*         Make recommendations on improvements that can be made
throughout the system 

*         Implement processes and controls to maintain integrity of the
system 

*         Implement technology to facilitate near real-time monitoring
of the systems performance characteristics 

*         Perform updates to the configuration of the various servers
and applications that comprise the system 

*         Assist in the deployment of new versions of the applications
that reside on the system 

*         On call rotation pager required full time 

 

Skills

*         Linux, RHEL, CentOS 

*         Apache, JBoss, MySQL 

*         Experience with F5 Load Balancers, Cisco/Force10 switching,
Juniper firewalls 

*         BGP, OSFP, DNS, SMTP, SNMP 

*         Experience working with Open Source Software 

*         Experience with Ganglia, Cacti, OpenNMS, Nagios monitoring
packages 

*         Ability to rapidly learn new technologies

 

*         Someone who is familiar with open source software

*         Someone who has worked with (40-100 servers)

*         Linux and Redhat experience 

*         5-6+ yrs experience 

*         Will be working on several projects 

*         Do not want a job hopper unless there is a really good reason
for it (i.e. family, education, etc.)

*         Recent college graduates will be considered.
