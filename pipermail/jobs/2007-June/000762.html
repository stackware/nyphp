TANDEMSEVEN JOB DESCRIPTION

 

 

TITLE:  PRINCIPAL TECHNICAL ARCHITECT

 

LOCATION: NYC AREA

RESPOND BY FORWARDING RESUME TO: ilevitt at tandemseven.com 

LEARN MORE ABOUT TANDEMSEVEN:   www.tandemseven.com

 

 

TandemSeven is a firm that designs and architects business portals and
e-business Applications specializing in creating usable, intuitive
interfaces for complex applications.  

 

POSITION DESCRIPTION

 

This Principal Technical Architect position requires a candidate who is
not only skilled in building technical solutions, but also has strong
experience working with clients, planning projects, writing proposals
and working with a seasoned sales team to generate new business
opportunities. 

 

While we require an unrestricted ability to travel, TandemSeven would
like to anchor technical teams around Principal Architects in the
Chicago and New York areas.

 

Required Skills:

 

Project Leadership

Able to lead engagements and work independently or with a team

Strong client-facing presentation and communication skills (both written
and verbal)

Able to plan projects, write proposals and statements of work 

 

Architectural

Requirements definition: gathering, documentation and tracking.
Experience working with a senior design team a plus.

Ability to design and deliver enterprise level solutions.

Able to handle multiple projects and proposals concurrently 

Must be able to work in the United States and travel to customer
engagements.  

Experience with UML is a plus.

 

Background/Experience

Minimum 7 years experience practicing software or web-based development.

The successful candidate should have a combination of Client, Server and
Database skills.  While no candidate will have all applicable skills,
they should be familiar enough with the architectures to lead experts in
the development of applications.

Client side skills:

		*	JSP/Struts/ASP/XML/XSLT/Swing/HTML/Java Script 
		*	Java Servlets 
		*	Portal IViews

Server side skills:

		*	Webservices, Services Oriented Architecture
(SOA) 
		*	J2EE, Java Certifications 
		*	Weblogic/Websphere/Open Source (JBOS, ANT,
Apache, Tomcat)

Database:

		*	Oracle (preferred) 
		*	SQLServer

Portal Experience:

		*	IBM Websphere 
		*	BEA

 

PREFERRED:

 

Minimum 5 years of senior consulting experience to large, Fortune 500
companies is preferred

 

New York candidates with Financial Services background will be given
first priority, as will Chicago candidates with Portal experience.

 

Experience and desire to create intellectual capital preferred (e.g.,
white papers, conference presentations, etc.)

 

 As part of TandemSeven's standard employment process, candidates will
undergo a complete background check prior to an offer being extended.
These background checks are conducted by a professional third party firm
at no charge to the applicant and include:  prior employment
verification, education verification, social security trace, criminal
background check and motor vehicles records (where required for
position).

 

TandemSeven Supports Workforce Diversity
