Job Description

We're hiring at Mobcaster for a Lead Developer. Mobcaster is the first and only crowd-funded marketplace for independent television. Mobcaster combines a crowd-funding platform and a market-curated online television channel to create the only destination to find and watch original, independent TV shows.

You will be joining a small, fast moving team. With an emphasis on forward thinking innovation and a fun start-up culture, the position will have ample opportunity for growth and involvement in many key aspects of the company's operations.

Skills & Requirements

Minimum Qualifications:
2+ years of professional PHP experience (able to write a web application from scratch in PHP & HTML/CSS/Javascript) - Symfony2 experience ideal
2+ years experience with MySQL and database design
Examples of fully built web application(s)
Experience in server monitoring, performance, and maintenance in a cloud environment (Rackspace/Amazon)
Be located in the Greater NYC Tri State Area. No relocation offered
Applicants must work well in a collaborative working environment and actively encourage and spearhead new ideas. They should have a preoccupation with staying on top of industry trends and technologies and be able to communicate clearly and professionally both in writing and in person.

Responsibilities:
Review, support and develop new web applications, features, and functionality
Monitor overall site performance, stability, and health
Identify and solve compatibility issues
Implement front-end UI for web applications
Work closely with back-end team to profile and optimize code
Work closely with clients, QA testing and support
Salary is competitive with industry standards.

About Mobcaster Inc.

Mobcaster is the first and only crowd-funded television channel. It combines a crowd-funding platform and a market-curated online television channel to create the only destination to find and watch original, independent TV shows.

Our mission is to empower TV makers and audiences. Mobcaster enables aspiring and experienced TV makers to pitch their shows directly out to the audience, and gives the audience the ability to dictate which shows ultimately get produced. By creating an independent pipeline to bring new TV shows to market, Mobcaster is able to eliminate the roadblocks of the traditional TV system, and revolutionize the way TV is created and consumed.

How to apply
