Broken Authentication and Session Management
OWASP Top 10 Web Application PHP Security Risks Monthly Series
--------------------------------------------------------------

 When: September 20th, 2011 at 6:30PM (sharp)
Where: Suspenders Restaurant, 111 Broadway, New York, NY

management.
