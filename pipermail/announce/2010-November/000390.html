PHP is built to voyage to the uncharted territories of the Internet.  Yet
failure to correctly navigate the latest trends, techniques and talk, can
send development adrift with a wayward strategy and loss of direction.

This past month, NYPHP pioneers Alan Seiden and Neveo Harrison made the
westward pilgrimage to the <a href="http://zendcon.com/">Zend/PHP Conference

back with them tales of untold fortune.

undiscovered - with Alan and Neveo leading us through the unexplored worlds
of internet application development.
