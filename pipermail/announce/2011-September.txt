ZendCon 2011
Early bird and NYPHP discounts available
----------------------------------------

ZendCon is the largest gathering of the PHP Community and a pillar in the
world of PHP conferences, bringing together PHP developers and IT managers
from around the world.

Early bird discount ends September 10th!

Plus NYPHP members receive a 10% discount - use code NYPHP.

Learn more and register:  http://www.zendcon.com/



Broken Authentication and Session Management
OWASP Top 10 Web Application PHP Security Risks Monthly Series
--------------------------------------------------------------

 When: September 20th, 2011 at 6:30PM (sharp)
Where: Suspenders Restaurant, 111 Broadway, New York, NY

New York PHP Community, in collaboration with OWASP, is holding a monthly
series that reviews each of the OWASP Top Ten Web Security threats.

As we enter the final months of 2011, we welcome back Anthony Ferrara to
share his insight and expertise on broken authentication and session
management.

   Full details and RSVP: http://www.nyphp.org/PHP-Presentations/193



Introducing the Joomla! Platform for Web and CLI Applications
Review of the Joomla! PHP Framework
-------------------------------------------------------------

 When: September 27th, 2011 at 6:30PM (sharp)
Where: IBM, 590 Madison Avenue, New York, NY (12th Floor)

After a heated summer, NYPHP is pleased to cool things down this Fall with a
look at a leading PHP framework, Joomla!. Join us this September as we
welcome Joomla New York leaders Elin Waring and Scott Wolpow to give us an
update and overview of this popular framework.

   Full details and RSVP: http://www.nyphp.org/PHP-Presentations/194
