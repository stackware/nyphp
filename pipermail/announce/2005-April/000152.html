New York PHP will be offering its well-known two-day in-depth training 
courses on both 16-17 May and 27-28 June.

The May course will be in our Programmer's Track, designed for developers 
with programming experience but no knowledge of PHP.  The June course will 
be in our Designer's Track, aimed at web designers with experience in HTML 
but no programming experience.

Full details, including information about early bird registration 
discounts, is at http://nyphp.org/twoday .
